# Cold Outreach Target Research — ARG Builder

## Research Sources
- Built In NYC/SF company listings
- LinkedIn job postings for VP Operations roles
- F6S company databases
- Industry-specific directories

## Target Criteria
- 200-2,000 employees (mid-market sweet spot)
- HQ or major office in NYC or SF
- Industries: Hospitality, Healthcare, Professional Services, Retail/E-Commerce, Manufacturing, SaaS
- Decision makers: VP Ops, COO, Director of Operations, Head of People, CTO

## Companies Identified

### Hospitality (NYC)
