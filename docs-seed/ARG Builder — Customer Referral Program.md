# ARG Builder — Customer Referral Program

**Purpose:** A structured referral program that turns happy founding customers into a scalable acquisition channel. Designed to launch after the first 5 customers are activated and showing positive results (Day 60+).

---

## Program Overview

The ARG Builder referral program rewards existing customers for introducing qualified mid-market companies to the platform. The program is intentionally generous during the founding phase to accelerate word-of-mouth growth within tight-knit operator communities in NYC and SF.

---

## Reward Structure

| Action | Referrer Gets | Referred Company Gets |
|--------|--------------|----------------------|
| Qualified introduction (books a demo) | $100 Amazon gift card | Priority demo slot + extended 21-day trial |
| Signed customer (Starter tier) | 1 month free ($299 credit) | 15% discount for first 12 months |
| Signed customer (Professional tier) | 2 months free (up to $750 credit) | 15% discount for first 12 months |
| Signed customer (Enterprise tier) | 3 months free (up to $7,500 credit) | Custom negotiation |

Rewards stack — there is no cap on the number of referrals a customer can make. A customer who refers 5 Professional-tier companies effectively gets 10 months free.

---

## Qualification Criteria

A "qualified introduction" means the referred company meets all of the following criteria: the company has 100+ employees (or is scaling toward that threshold within 6 months), they have a clear operational knowledge challenge (scattered docs, compliance gaps, onboarding issues), and the introduction is to a decision-maker or strong influencer (VP/Director level or above). Self-referrals, referrals to companies already in the pipeline, and referrals to direct competitors of ARG Builder do not qualify.

---

## How It Works

**Step 1: Customer makes an introduction.** The referrer sends a warm email introduction connecting Jamal with the prospect. Alternatively, they can share a unique referral link that tracks attribution automatically.

**Step 2: Qualification.** ARG Builder confirms the referral meets qualification criteria within 2 business days and notifies the referrer.

**Step 3: Demo and close.** ARG Builder runs the standard sales process (30-minute demo, proposal, close). The referrer is kept informed of progress via brief status updates.

**Step 4: Reward delivery.** For qualified introductions, the $100 gift card is delivered within 5 business days of the demo occurring. For signed customers, the account credit is applied to the referrer's next billing cycle within 10 business days of the new customer's first payment.

## Tracking and Attribution

Referrals are tracked through three mechanisms. First, warm email introductions where the referrer CCs or introduces Jamal directly — these are manually attributed in the CRM. Second, unique referral links (`argbuilder.io/r/[customer-slug]`) that automatically tag the lead source in the pipeline. Third, the referrer can submit referrals through a simple form in their account settings under "Refer a Friend."

All referral activity, reward status, and payout history is visible to the customer in their account dashboard under Settings → Referrals.

---

## Program Economics

At a $100 cost per qualified demo and 1–3 months free per converted customer, the effective CAC through referrals is approximately $400–$750 — significantly below the target CAC of $1,500 for direct outbound. Referral-sourced customers also typically have 40% higher retention rates (per SaaS industry benchmarks from OpenView Partners) because they enter with social proof and realistic expectations.

The program becomes ROI-positive after 2 referral-sourced customers at the Professional tier (break-even on reward costs within the first billing cycle of the referred customer).
