# ARG-Builder: Investor Relations Dashboard

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's Investor Relations Dashboard — the metrics, reporting cadence, communication framework, and data visualization strategy that keeps investors informed, engaged, and confident. A well-structured IR dashboard transforms investor updates from a chore into a strategic advantage, building trust through transparency and demonstrating operational excellence through data-driven storytelling.

---

## Dashboard Architecture

### Metric Hierarchy

| Level | Audience | Metrics | Update Frequency |
|-------|----------|---------|-----------------|
| **Executive Summary** | All investors | ARR, growth rate, runway, NRR | Monthly |
| **Operational Detail** | Board + lead investors | Pipeline, CAC, LTV, churn, headcount | Monthly |
| **Deep Dive** | Board only | Unit economics, cohort analysis, product metrics | Quarterly |
| **Ad Hoc** | On request | Custom analysis, scenario modeling | As needed |

## Core Metrics Dashboard

### Revenue & Growth

| Metric | Definition | Target | Visualization |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------|
| ARR | Annual Recurring Revenue (current MRR × 12) | $2.2M Year 1 → $25.6M Year 5 | Line chart (monthly) |
| MRR | Monthly Recurring Revenue | Tracked monthly | Bar chart |
| ARR growth rate (YoY) | (Current ARR - Prior Year ARR) / Prior Year ARR | > 100% Year 1–2, > 80% Year 3 | Line chart |
| Net new ARR | New + Expansion - Contraction - Churn | Increasing quarterly | Waterfall chart |
| Revenue mix | Subscription vs. services vs. other | > 85% subscription | Pie chart |
| ARPA (Avg Revenue Per Account) | Total ARR / # customers | $48K → $72K | Line chart |

### ARR Waterfall (Monthly)

| Component | Definition | Target |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Beginning ARR | Start of period | — |
| + New ARR | Revenue from new customers | Growing monthly |
| + Expansion ARR | Upsells, seat additions, tier upgrades | > 30% of new ARR |
| - Contraction ARR | Downgrades, seat reductions | < 5% of beginning ARR |
| - Churned ARR | Lost customers | < 8% annually (logo), < 5% (revenue) |
| = Ending ARR | End of period | Higher than beginning |

### Efficiency Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target | Benchmark |
|--------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| CAC (blended) | Total S&M spend / new customers acquired | < $15K | Top quartile: < $12K |
| CAC payback (months) | CAC / (ARPA × gross margin) / 12 | < 12 months | Top quartile: < 8 months |
| LTV | ARPA × gross margin × (1 / annual churn rate) | > $300K | Top quartile: > $500K |
| LTV/CAC ratio | LTV / CAC | > 5x | Top quartile: > 7x |
| Magic number | Net new ARR / prior quarter S&M spend | > 0.75 | Top quartile: > 1.0 |
| Burn multiple | Net burn / net new ARR | < 2.0x | Top quartile: < 1.5x |
| Rule of 40 | Revenue growth % + FCF margin % | > 40% | Top quartile: > 60% |

### Customer Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target | Visualization |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Total customers | Active paying accounts | Growing monthly | Line chart |
| Net Revenue Retention (NRR) | (Beginning ARR + expansion - contraction - churn) / Beginning ARR | > 120% | Line chart |
| Gross Revenue Retention (GRR) | (Beginning ARR - contraction - churn) / Beginning ARR | > 90% | Line chart |
| Logo churn (monthly) | Customers lost / beginning customers | < 1.5%/month | Line chart |
| Customer health score | Weighted composite (usage, engagement, satisfaction) | > 75 average | Distribution chart |
| NPS | Net Promoter Score | > 50 | Gauge chart |

## Monthly Investor Update

### Update Structure

| Section | Content | Length |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **Headline** | One sentence: biggest win or milestone | 1 line |
| **Key metrics** | ARR, growth, runway, customers, NRR | 5–6 numbers |
| **Wins** | Top 3 achievements this month | 3 bullets |
| **Challenges** | Top 2 challenges (with plan to address) | 2 bullets |
| **Asks** | Specific help needed from investors | 1–3 bullets |
| **Looking ahead** | Key priorities for next month | 3 bullets |

### Monthly Update Template

> **Subject:** ARG-Builder Monthly Update — [Month Year] | ARR: $[X]M | +[Y]% MoM
>
> **Headline:** [One sentence biggest achievement]
>
> **Key Metrics:**
> | Metric | This Month | Last Month | Target |
> |--------|-----------|-----------|--------|
> | ARR | $X | $Y | $Z |
> | MRR Growth | X% | Y% | Z% |
> | Customers | X | Y | Z |
> | NRR | X% | Y% | > 120% |
> | Runway | X months | Y months | > 18 months |
> | Cash | $X | $Y | — |
>
> **Wins:**
> 1. [Win 1 with specific detail]
> 2. [Win 2 with specific detail]
> 3. [Win 3 with specific detail]
>
> **Challenges:**
> 1. [Challenge + what we're doing about it]

>

> 1. [Priority 1]

## Quarterly Board Reporting

### Financial Dashboard

| Report | Content | Format |
|--------|---------|--------|
| P&L (actual vs. budget vs. prior year) | Revenue, COGS, gross margin, OpEx by department, EBITDA | Table + variance commentary |
| Cash flow | Operating, investing, financing activities | Waterfall chart |
| Balance sheet | Assets, liabilities, equity | Table |
| ARR bridge | Beginning → New → Expansion → Contraction → Churn → Ending | Waterfall chart |
| Runway analysis | Monthly burn × months of cash remaining | Line chart with scenarios |
| Budget variance | Actual vs. plan by department | Table (red/yellow/green) |

### Operational Dashboard

| Report | Content | Format |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Sales pipeline | Stage-by-stage pipeline, velocity, conversion | Funnel chart |
| Win/loss analysis | Top reasons for wins and losses | Bar chart + commentary |
| Customer cohort analysis | Retention by cohort, expansion by cohort | Cohort table (heat map) |
| Product usage | Feature adoption, DAU/MAU, engagement | Line charts |
| Team metrics | Headcount, hiring velocity, attrition | Table |
| OKR progress | Company OKRs with status (on track / at risk / off track) | Scorecard |
