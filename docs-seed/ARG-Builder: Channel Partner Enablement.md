# ARG-Builder: Channel Partner Enablement

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's channel partner enablement program — the comprehensive training, certification, tools, and support framework that empowers partners to successfully sell, implement, and support ARG-Builder solutions. Effective enablement transforms partners from passive referrers into active, competent extensions of the sales and delivery team.

---

## Enablement Architecture

### Program Structure

| Level | Name | Requirements | Capabilities Unlocked | Revenue Share |
|-------|------|-------------|----------------------|---------------|

## Co-Selling Framework

### Engagement Models

| Model | When to Use | Partner Role | ARG-Builder Role | Revenue Split |
|-------|-------------|-------------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Referral** | Partner identifies opportunity, passes to us | Introduce, warm handoff | Full sales cycle | 15% to partner |
| **Co-sell** | Joint selling with shared responsibilities | Discovery, relationship, industry expertise | Demo, technical, closing | 20–25% to partner |
| **Partner-led** | Partner owns full sales cycle | Full sales + implementation | Technical support, escalation | 25–30% to partner |
| **White-label** | Partner resells under their brand | Everything customer-facing | Platform, support (behind scenes) | 30–35% to partner |

### Deal Registration

| Element | Policy |
|---------|--------|
| Registration window | 90 days exclusive (extendable to 180 for enterprise) |
| Approval SLA | 48 hours for registration approval |
| Conflict resolution | First to register wins; if simultaneous, partner with stronger relationship |
| Minimum deal size | $20K ACV for registration protection |
| Reporting | Monthly pipeline review with partner manager |
