# Riad & Routes — Driver Dispatch & Fleet Management

## 1. Purpose

This SOP covers the daily dispatch of drivers for airport transfers, tour transportation, and ad-hoc guest requests. Reliable, punctual transport is non-negotiable in luxury hospitality — a late driver or dirty vehicle instantly undermines the entire guest experience.

## 4. Daily Dispatch Process

### 4.1 Evening Planning (20:00 – Previous Day)

The Transport Coordinator reviews the next day's schedule:

1. Pull all confirmed transfers and tours from the booking system
2. Assign drivers based on: vehicle requirement, language match, route familiarity, shift availability
3. Post the dispatch board (physical whiteboard in driver room + digital in WhatsApp group)
4. Send individual confirmations to each driver: pickup time, guest name, destination, vehicle assigned, special notes

### 4.2 Morning Confirmation (06:30)

Each driver confirms readiness via WhatsApp:
- "Vehicle checked ✓" (cleanliness, fuel, AC, water bottles stocked)
- "En route to property" or "Standing by at [location]"

If a driver fails to confirm by 06:45, the Transport Coordinator calls immediately. If unreachable, the backup driver is activated.

### 4.3 Dispatch Execution

For each trip:

| Phase | Driver Action | Coordinator Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Departure from base | Send "Departing" message with ETA | Log departure time |
| Arrival at pickup | Send "Arrived" + photo of location | Notify guest/front desk |
| Guest on board | Send "Guest collected, en route" | Log pickup time |
| Arrival at destination | Send "Arrived at [destination]" | Log arrival, notify relevant team |
| Return to base | Send "Returning" with ETA | Update availability board |

### 4.4 Airport Transfer Specifics

**Arrivals:**
- Driver must arrive at airport 30 minutes before flight lands
- Park in designated waiting area (not in taxi queue)
- Hold branded name sign (black leather folder with guest name in gold lettering)
- Monitor flight status via FlightAware — if delayed, adjust arrival accordingly
- Maximum wait time after landing: 90 minutes (then contact coordinator for instructions)

**Departures:**
- Pickup from property 3 hours before international flights, 2.5 hours before domestic
- Driver assists with luggage loading and confirms nothing left in vehicle
- Walk guest to departure terminal entrance (do not just drop at curb)

## 6. Vehicle Presentation Standards

Every vehicle must meet these standards before any guest trip:

- **Exterior:** Spotless, no dust, no scratches visible. Wheels clean.
- **Interior:** No odors (no air freshener — neutral scent only). Leather conditioned. No crumbs or debris.
- **Amenities:** 2 cold water bottles (branded), pack of tissues, phone charger (Lightning + USB-C), WiFi hotspot charged.
- **Temperature:** AC pre-cooled to 22°C in summer, heated to 22°C in winter. Vehicle must be running 10 minutes before guest boards.
- **Music:** Ambient Moroccan instrumental at low volume. Guest can request change or silence.

## 7. Emergency Protocols

| Scenario | Immediate Action | Escalation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Vehicle breakdown | Pull over safely, inform coordinator, comfort guest | Backup vehicle dispatched within 30 minutes |
| Traffic accident (minor) | Exchange details, photograph damage, continue if safe | Report to Operations Manager within 1 hour |
| Traffic accident (serious) | Call emergency services, secure guests, do not move vehicle | GM notified immediately, insurance activated |
| Guest medical emergency | Drive to nearest hospital (Clinique Internationale preferred) | Coordinator notifies GM + guest's emergency contact |
| Road closure/protest | Reroute using local knowledge, inform coordinator | Update guest on delay, offer refreshments |

## 8. Cost Control & Fuel Management

- All vehicles use assigned fuel cards (Shell or Afriquia stations only)
- Fuel receipts must be submitted weekly with odometer reading
- Monthly fuel budget per vehicle: 3,000 MAD (sedan), 4,500 MAD (SUV/van), 6,000 MAD (minibus)
- Any trip exceeding 200 km requires pre-approval from Operations Manager
- Personal use of vehicles is strictly prohibited
