## Partnership Value Proposition

ARG Builder's partner program is designed around a simple premise: consulting firms and HR platforms already serve mid-market companies struggling with operational knowledge chaos. By partnering with ARG Builder, they can offer a concrete solution to a problem their clients already have — while earning 20% recurring revenue share on every referral or co-selling the implementation services ($5K–$50K per engagement).

## Outreach Cadence

Send the initial email on Tuesday or Wednesday morning (10 AM local time). If no response after 5 business days, send the follow-up template. If still no response after another 5 days, connect on LinkedIn with a brief note referencing the email. After 3 touches with no response, move to a quarterly nurture cadence (share relevant content or case studies every 90 days).
