## Negotiation Framework

### BATNA Analysis (Pre-Negotiation)

| Element | Our Position | Their Likely Position | Walk-Away Point |
|---------|-------------|----------------------|-----------------|
| Price | List price | 20–30% discount | > 35% discount |
| Terms | Standard 1-year | Multi-year with discount | < 6 months |
| Payment | Annual upfront | Monthly or quarterly | Net 90+ |
| SLA | 99.9% standard | 99.99% with penalties | Unlimited liability |
| Scope | Standard features | Custom development | > 20% of roadmap |

### Negotiation Tactics by Buyer Type

| Buyer Type | Their Approach | Our Counter-Strategy |
|-----------|---------------|---------------------|
| **Procurement** | Squeeze on price, extend payment terms | Trade price for term length, emphasize TCO |
| **Legal** | Redline everything, expand liability | Offer mutual terms, reference industry standard |
| **IT/Security** | Focus on compliance, data requirements | Provide SOC 2 report, offer DPA, security questionnaire |
| **Executive** | Big picture value, strategic alignment | Focus on business outcomes, offer executive sponsorship |
| **Champion** | Wants to close quickly, needs internal ammo | Provide ROI calculator, case studies, reference calls |

## Procurement Navigation

### Enterprise Procurement Process

| Stage | Duration | Key Activities | Our Actions |
|-------|----------|---------------|-------------|
| Vendor registration | 1–2 weeks | Complete vendor forms, provide certifications | Submit promptly, provide all docs |
| Security review | 2–4 weeks | Security questionnaire, penetration test results | Provide SOC 2, pre-fill questionnaire |
| Legal review | 2–4 weeks | Contract redlines, DPA negotiation | Respond within 48 hours to redlines |
| Commercial negotiation | 1–2 weeks | Pricing, terms, SLA negotiation | Follow authority matrix |
| Final approval | 1–2 weeks | Internal sign-offs, PO generation | Provide any final documentation |
| Signature | 1 week | DocuSign or wet signature | Send immediately upon agreement |

### Common Procurement Blockers & Solutions

| Blocker | Solution | Prevention |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| Security questionnaire (200+ questions) | Pre-filled CAIQ, SOC 2 report, security page | Maintain up-to-date security documentation |
| Legal redlines on liability | Offer mutual cap at 12 months fees | Include clear limitation language upfront |
| Budget cycle mismatch | Offer flexible start dates, bridge pricing | Identify budget cycles early in sales process |
| Multiple stakeholder sign-off | Provide executive summary for each stakeholder | Map decision-making unit early |
| Competitive bake-off requirement | Offer guided POC with success criteria | Differentiate on value, not just features |
