# Riad & Routes — Guest Complaint Resolution Framework

## 1. Purpose

Complaints are inevitable in hospitality — what defines a luxury property is how swiftly and gracefully they are resolved. This framework ensures every complaint is treated as a gift: an opportunity to demonstrate our commitment to excellence and convert a dissatisfied guest into a loyal advocate. The goal is not just resolution but recovery that exceeds the original expectation.

## 2. The HEART Method

All staff are trained in the HEART complaint resolution method:

| Step | Action | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **H** — Hear | Listen fully without interrupting. Make eye contact. Put down whatever you're doing. | "I understand. Please tell me everything." |
| **E** — Empathize | Acknowledge the feeling, not just the fact. Validate their frustration. | "I completely understand how frustrating that must be." |
| **A** — Apologize | Sincere, specific apology. Own the issue on behalf of the property. | "I'm truly sorry that happened during your stay with us." |
| **R** — Resolve | Offer a concrete solution immediately. If you can't, escalate within 5 minutes. | "Let me fix this right now. I'm going to..." |
| **T** — Thank | Thank the guest for bringing it to your attention. Follow up to confirm satisfaction. | "Thank you for telling us. We'll make sure this doesn't happen again." |

## 3. Complaint Categories & Authority Levels

| Category | Examples | First Responder Authority | Escalation Trigger |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Minor inconvenience | Slow WiFi, missing amenity, cold food | Immediate fix + verbal apology | Guest still unhappy after fix |
| Service failure | Late transfer, wrong room type, booking error | Fix + complimentary item (up to 300 MAD value) | Guest requests manager |
| Serious issue | Noise at night, cleanliness failure, safety concern | Fix + comp service (up to 1,000 MAD) + manager visit | Any safety issue or repeat complaint |
| Critical failure | Health issue from food, injury on property, theft, privacy breach | GM immediate involvement + full investigation | Always escalated immediately |

## 4. Resolution Timeline

| Severity | Acknowledgment | Resolution | Follow-up |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Minor | Immediate (within 2 minutes) | Within 15 minutes | Same day check-in |
| Service failure | Immediate | Within 30 minutes | Next morning + departure |
| Serious | Within 5 minutes | Within 1 hour | Daily until departure |
| Critical | Immediate (GM notified within 5 minutes) | Active management until resolved | Post-departure call by GM |

## 5. Compensation Guidelines

| Situation | Standard Compensation | VIP Compensation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Room not ready at check-in | Complimentary drink + early check-in next stay | Immediate upgrade + spa credit |
| Noise complaint (verified) | Room move offer + complimentary breakfast | Room move + full comp night |
| Food quality issue | Replacement dish + dessert on the house | Full meal comped + chef's personal apology |
| Transfer delay (>15 min) | Complimentary minibar for the stay | Complimentary experience (tour/spa) |
| Housekeeping missed | Immediate service + amenity basket | Immediate service + comp turndown upgrade |
| Booking error (our fault) | Upgrade to best available + 20% off next stay | Full comp night + future stay discount |
| Health/safety incident | Full investigation + medical costs covered + comp stay | All above + GM personal follow-up + legal review |

## 6. Documentation Requirements

Every complaint must be logged in the Complaint Register (digital system) within 1 hour of resolution:

1. **Guest details:** Name, room number, VIP tier, stay dates
2. **Complaint details:** Date/time reported, category, description in guest's own words
3. **Staff involved:** Who received the complaint, who resolved it
4. **Root cause:** Why did this happen? (staffing, process, equipment, supplier, one-off)
5. **Resolution:** What was done, what was offered, guest's reaction
6. **Prevention:** What will we change to prevent recurrence?
7. **Cost:** Value of compensation provided
8. **Follow-up:** Confirmation that guest is satisfied post-resolution

## 7. Escalation Matrix

```
Guest reports issue
        │
        ▼
Staff member applies HEART method
        │
        ├── Resolved? → Log and follow up
        │
        ▼ (Not resolved in 5 min)
Front Desk Manager takes over
        │
        ├── Resolved? → Log and follow up
        │
        ▼ (Not resolved in 15 min OR serious/critical)
General Manager contacted
        │
        ├── Resolved? → Log, follow up, debrief team
        │
        ▼ (Legal/safety/PR risk)
Owner notified + external support (legal, PR, medical)
```

## 8. Online Review Response Protocol

If a complaint appears on TripAdvisor, Google, or Booking.com before internal resolution:

1. **Within 4 hours:** Guest Relations Manager drafts a response (never defensive, always empathetic)
2. **Response template:** "Dear [Name], thank you for sharing your experience. We sincerely apologize for [specific issue]. This does not reflect our standards. Our [GM/Manager] would love to speak with you directly — please contact us at [email]. We are committed to making this right."
3. **Within 24 hours:** Attempt direct contact with the guest to resolve offline
4. **If resolved:** Politely ask if they would consider updating their review
5. **Monthly:** GM reviews all negative reviews in team meeting to identify patterns

## 9. Prevention & Learning

### 9.1 Weekly Complaint Review (Every Monday, 09:00)

The management team reviews all complaints from the previous week:
- Pattern identification (same issue recurring = systemic problem)
- Staff recognition (excellent complaint handling highlighted)
- Process changes implemented and tracked
- Training needs identified

### 9.2 Complaint Reduction Targets

| Quarter | Target | Measurement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Q1 2026 | < 5 complaints per 100 guest-nights | Complaint register / occupancy |
| Q2 2026 | < 4 complaints per 100 guest-nights | Complaint register / occupancy |
| Q3 2026 | < 3 complaints per 100 guest-nights | Complaint register / occupancy |
| Q4 2026 | < 2 complaints per 100 guest-nights | Complaint register / occupancy |

### 9.3 Recovery Success Rate

Target: 85% of guests who complained rate their overall stay 4+ stars on the post-stay survey. This means our recovery exceeded their original expectation.
