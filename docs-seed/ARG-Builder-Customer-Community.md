## 3. Community Platform & Structure

### 3.1 Platform Selection

| Platform | Use Case | Pros | Cons |
|----------|----------|------|------|
| Slack (Private workspace) | Real-time discussion | Familiar, low friction | Noisy, hard to search |
| Circle | Structured community | Organized, searchable | Another login |
| Discourse | Forum-style discussion | Deep threads, SEO | Feels dated |
| Custom (in-product) | Embedded community | Seamless, data-rich | Build cost |

**Recommendation:** Start with Slack (low friction, fast launch), migrate to Circle or custom at 500+ members.
