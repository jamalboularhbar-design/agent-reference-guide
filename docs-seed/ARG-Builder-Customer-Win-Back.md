# ARG-Builder: Customer Win-Back Program

## Systematic Framework for Re-Engaging Churned Customers and Recovering Lost Revenue

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Winning back churned customers is 5–7x more cost-effective than acquiring new ones — they already know the product, have existing data, and require less education. A structured win-back program can recover 10–25% of churned accounts within 12 months when executed with proper timing, personalization, and value demonstration. This document defines ARG-Builder's complete win-back strategy from segmentation through re-engagement to measurement.

## 3. Win-Back Timing

### 3.1 Optimal Timing Windows

| Window | Timing Post-Churn | Rationale | Approach |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|----------|
| Immediate | 1–7 days | Cancellation regret, save attempt | Personal call, retention offer |
| Early | 30–60 days | Still remember product, may miss it | Check-in email, value reminder |
| Feature trigger | When relevant feature ships | Direct solution to their stated need | Product update announcement |
| Renewal anniversary | 12 months post-churn | Budget cycle reset, fresh evaluation | Re-engagement campaign |
| Competitive fatigue | 6–9 months | Honeymoon with competitor ending | Competitive comparison content |
| Industry trigger | Event-driven | Regulatory change, market shift | Thought leadership + relevance |

### 3.2 Timing Rules

| Rule | Implementation |
|------|---------------|
| Respect cooling-off period | No outreach in first 14 days (unless they reach out) |
| Maximum 6 touches in 12 months | Avoid spam perception |
| Stop on unsubscribe | Immediately honor opt-out |
| Re-engage on signal | Website visit, content download, feature request |
| Annual review | After 18 months with no engagement, archive |
| Competitive intelligence | Monitor for dissatisfaction signals |

## 5. Win-Back Offers

### 5.1 Offer Framework

| Offer Type | Applicable Segment | Discount | Duration | Conditions |
|-----------|-------------------|----------|----------|-----------|
| Welcome back discount | All (except business closed) | 20–30% off | 3 months | Annual commitment |
| Extended trial | Feature-gap, low-adoption | 30–60 days free | Trial period | No commitment |
| Plan downgrade | Budget-constrained | Lower tier access | Ongoing | Reduced features |
| White-glove onboarding | Low-adoption | Free implementation ($5K value) | One-time | 12-month contract |
| Migration assistance | Competitive switch | Free data migration | One-time | Annual commitment |
| Custom package | High-value accounts | Negotiated | Custom | Custom terms |
| Loyalty pricing | Long-tenure churned (12+ months prior) | Lock in old pricing | 12 months | Annual commitment |

### 5.2 Offer Approval Matrix

| Offer Value | Approval Required | Approver |
|-------------|------------------|----------|
| < $5K discount | CSM/AE autonomy | Self-approve |
| $5K–$15K discount | Manager approval | CS/Sales Manager |
| $15K–$50K discount | VP approval | VP CS or VP Sales |
| > $50K discount or custom terms | Executive approval | CRO or CEO |
