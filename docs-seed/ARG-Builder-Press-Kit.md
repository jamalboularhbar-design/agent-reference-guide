## 4. Product Information

### 4.1 Product Description

**What ARG-Builder Does:**

ARG-Builder is an AI-powered platform that autonomously generates complete operational reference systems for businesses. Instead of spending months manually creating documentation, companies describe their operations and ARG-Builder generates comprehensive, interconnected operational guides — including SOPs, playbooks, training materials, compliance documentation, and process frameworks.

**How It Works:**

1. **Describe** — Tell ARG-Builder about your business, team structure, and operational needs
2. **Generate** — AI creates a complete operational reference system in hours
3. **Customize** — Review, refine, and personalize the generated content
4. **Evolve** — The system continuously updates as your business changes
5. **Measure** — Track adoption, usage, and operational improvement metrics

**Key Capabilities:**
- Autonomous operational content generation
- Intelligent knowledge organization and cross-referencing
- Continuous content evolution and freshness management
- Role-based access and personalized views
- Compliance mapping and audit readiness
- Integration with existing business tools
- Analytics and operational intelligence reporting
