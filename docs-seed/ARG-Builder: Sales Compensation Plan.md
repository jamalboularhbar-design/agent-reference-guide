# ARG-Builder: Sales Compensation Plan

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's sales compensation plan — the quota structures, commission rates, accelerators, SPIFs, and governance policies that attract top sales talent, align incentives with company goals, and drive predictable revenue growth. A well-designed comp plan is the single most powerful lever for shaping sales behavior. This plan is designed for a B2B SaaS company selling $24K–$120K+ ACV deals to mid-market and enterprise customers.

---

## Compensation Philosophy

### Guiding Principles

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|---------------|
| Pay for performance | 50/50 base/variable split for quota-carrying reps |
| Uncapped earnings | No commission caps — reward overperformance |
| Simplicity | Maximum 3 commission components per role |
| Alignment | Comp rewards behaviors that drive long-term company value |
| Fairness | Transparent, consistent, equitable across team |
| Competitiveness | Target 75th percentile OTE for market |

## Commission Structure

### Standard Commission (AE)

| Attainment | Commission Rate | Multiplier | Example ($800K quota) |
|-----------|----------------|-----------|----------------------|
| 0–50% | 5% (reduced rate) | 0.5x | $20K on $400K |
| 51–100% | 10% (standard rate) | 1.0x | $40K on next $400K |
| 101–125% | 15% (accelerator 1) | 1.5x | $30K on next $200K |
| 126–150% | 20% (accelerator 2) | 2.0x | $40K on next $200K |
| 151%+ | 25% (super accelerator) | 2.5x | Uncapped |

### Commission Triggers

| Event | Commission Paid | Timing |
|-------|----------------|--------|
| New business closed-won | Full commission on Year 1 ACV | 50% at contract signing, 50% at first payment |
| Multi-year deal | Commission on Year 1 ACV + 50% of Year 2+ ACV | Same as above |
| Expansion (existing customer) | 8% of expansion ACV | At booking |
| Renewal (at or above rate) | 2% of renewal ACV | At renewal signing |
| Professional services | 5% of services revenue | At delivery completion |

## Quota Setting

### Quota Methodology

| Factor | Consideration | Weight |
|--------|--------------|--------|
| Company ARR target | Top-down from board-approved plan | 40% |
| Territory potential | Bottom-up from addressable accounts | 30% |
| Historical performance | Prior year attainment, ramp curves | 20% |
| Market conditions | Industry growth, competitive dynamics | 10% |

### Quota Ramp (New Hire)

| Month | Quota % | Rationale |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| Month 1 | 0% | Training, onboarding, shadowing |
| Month 2 | 0% | Territory planning, prospecting begins |
| Month 3 | 25% | Pipeline building, first meetings |
| Month 4 | 50% | Active selling, pipeline maturing |
| Month 5 | 75% | Approaching full productivity |
| Month 6+ | 100% | Fully ramped |

### Quota Allocation (Annual)

| Quarter | % of Annual Quota | Rationale |
|---------|------------------|-----------|
| Q1 | 20% | Post-holiday ramp, budget season |
| Q2 | 25% | Full speed, new budgets available |
| Q3 | 25% | Steady state |
| Q4 | 30% | Year-end budget flush, urgency |

## Deal Economics & Discounting

### Discount Authority

| Discount Level | Approver | Conditions |
|---------------|----------|-----------|
| 0–10% | AE (self-approved) | Standard negotiation |
| 11–20% | Sales Manager | Business justification required |
| 21–30% | VP Sales | Strategic deal memo required |
| 31%+ | CEO | Exceptional circumstances only |

### Commission Impact of Discounting

| Discount Given | Commission Adjustment | Rationale |
|---------------|---------------------|-----------|
| 0–10% | Full commission | Normal negotiation |
| 11–20% | 90% of commission | Slight reduction to discourage |
| 21–30% | 75% of commission | Significant reduction |
| 31%+ | 50% of commission | Strong disincentive |

## Governance & Administration

### Plan Administration

| Process | Frequency | Owner | Participants |
|---------|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Quota setting | Annually (November) | VP Sales + Finance | CEO, VP Sales, Finance |
| Plan design review | Annually (December) | VP Sales + HR | CEO, VP Sales, HR, Finance |
| Commission calculation | Monthly | Sales Ops / Finance | Automated + manual review |
| Commission payment | Monthly (by 15th) | Finance | Payroll |
| Dispute resolution | As needed | VP Sales | AE, Manager, Sales Ops |
| Plan communication | Annually (January) | VP Sales | All sales team |

### Clawback Policy

| Scenario | Clawback | Timeline |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Customer churns within 3 months | 100% of commission returned | Deducted from next payment |
| Customer churns within 6 months | 50% of commission returned | Deducted from next payment |
| Customer downgrades within 3 months | Difference returned | Deducted from next payment |
| Deal terms materially changed post-close | Adjusted to actual terms | Deducted from next payment |
| Fraudulent deal (misrepresentation) | 100% returned + disciplinary | Immediate |
