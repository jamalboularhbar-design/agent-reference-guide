# ARG-Builder: Customer Community Platform

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's customer community platform strategy — the structure, engagement model, content strategy, and growth plan for building a thriving community of operations professionals who use ARG-Builder to transform their organizations. A well-built community reduces support costs, increases retention, drives organic acquisition, and creates a defensible moat through network effects.

---

## Community Architecture

### Platform Structure

| Space | Purpose | Access | Moderation |
|-------|---------|--------|-----------|
| **General Discussion** | Broad conversation, introductions, announcements | All members | Light |
| **Best Practices** | Proven strategies, templates, workflows | All members | Curated |
| **Product Feedback** | Feature requests, bug reports, roadmap input | All members | Product team monitors |
| **Industry Groups** | Vertical-specific discussions (SaaS, Finance, Healthcare, etc.) | Self-select | Community managers |
| **Use Case Library** | Documented implementations, before/after stories | All members (contribute = verified customers) | Curated |
| **Office Hours** | Live Q&A with product team, experts | All members | Scheduled |
| **Champions Circle** | Exclusive space for power users and advocates | Invitation only | Dedicated manager |
| **Partner Hub** | Implementation partners, integrators | Verified partners | Partner manager |

### Membership Tiers

| Tier | Criteria | Benefits | Recognition |
|------|----------|----------|-------------|
| **Member** | Active ARG-Builder customer | Full community access, ask questions, share | Standard badge |
| **Contributor** | 10+ helpful posts, 3+ resources shared | Early access to features, contributor badge | Bronze badge |
| **Expert** | 50+ posts, recognized expertise, helps others | Direct product team access, beta testing | Silver badge |
| **Champion** | Top 5% engagement, active advocate, case study | Champions Circle, swag, conference invites | Gold badge |
| **Ambassador** | External advocacy, speaking, content creation | Revenue share on referrals, co-marketing | Platinum badge |
