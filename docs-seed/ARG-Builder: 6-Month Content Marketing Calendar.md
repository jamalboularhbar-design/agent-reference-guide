# ARG-Builder: 6-Month Content Marketing Calendar

## Content Strategy Overview

ARG-Builder's content marketing strategy positions the company as the definitive authority on operational intelligence for mid-market companies. Content serves three purposes: attract qualified prospects through SEO and thought leadership, nurture leads through educational content, and convert prospects through proof-of-value content.

### Content Pillars

| Pillar | Purpose | Content Types | Frequency |
|--------|---------|---------------|-----------|
| **Operational Excellence** | Attract mid-market operations leaders | Blog posts, guides, research | 3x/month |
| **AI & Automation** | Position as technology innovator | Thought leadership, technical posts | 2x/month |
| **Customer Success** | Build credibility and social proof | Case studies, testimonials, metrics | 2x/month |
| **Industry Insights** | Demonstrate domain expertise | Industry reports, benchmarks, trends | 1x/month |

### Distribution Channels

| Channel | Content Type | Frequency | KPI |
|---------|-------------|-----------|-----|
| Blog | Long-form articles (1,500–3,000 words) | 2x/week | Organic traffic, time on page |
| LinkedIn | Thought leadership, company updates | 5x/week | Engagement, followers, leads |
| Email Newsletter | Curated insights, product updates | 1x/week | Open rate, click rate |
| Webinars | Educational presentations | 2x/month | Registrations, attendees, SQLs |
| YouTube | Product demos, customer stories | 2x/month | Views, subscribers |
| Podcast | Industry interviews, deep dives | 2x/month | Downloads, reviews |

| Friday | Scheduling and publication | Content Lead |

### Content Quality Standards

Every piece of content must meet the following criteria before publication:

1. **Accuracy:** All statistics and claims are verified and cited
2. **Value:** Provides actionable insight the reader can implement immediately
3. **Originality:** Offers a unique perspective not available elsewhere
4. **Readability:** Flesch-Kincaid score of 60+ (readable by general business audience)
5. **SEO:** Optimized for target keyword with proper meta tags and structure
6. **Brand:** Consistent with voice, tone, and visual identity guidelines
7. **CTA:** Clear next step for the reader (download, register, contact, share)
