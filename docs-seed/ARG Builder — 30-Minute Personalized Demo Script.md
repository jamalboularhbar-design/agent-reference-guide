# ARG Builder — 30-Minute Personalized Demo Script

**Format:** Live Zoom/Google Meet with screen share
**Duration:** 30 minutes (structured as 5-20-5)
**Audience:** VP/Director of Operations, HR, or CTO at mid-market company
**Goal:** Secure a 14-day pilot commitment or Founding Customer signup

---

## Pre-Demo Preparation (5 minutes before)

Before each demo, prepare these:

1. Research the prospect's company on LinkedIn — note team size, recent hires, industry
2. Pre-load 3–5 sample documents relevant to their vertical (e.g., hospitality SOPs for a hotel group, onboarding playbooks for a SaaS company)
3. Have the knowledge graph populated with their industry terminology
4. Open their website in a tab for reference during conversation
5. Have the ROI calculator pre-filled with their approximate numbers

---

## Part 1: Discovery (5 minutes)

**Objective:** Understand their pain, establish credibility, set the agenda.

> "Thanks for taking the time, [Name]. Before I show you anything, I'd love to understand how your team currently handles operational documentation. Can you walk me through what happens when a new team member needs to find a process or SOP?"

**Follow-up questions (pick 2–3 based on their answers):**

- "How many tools does your team currently use to store operational knowledge?"
- "When was the last time someone followed an outdated procedure and it caused an issue?"
- "If I asked 5 people on your team where to find [specific process], would they all look in the same place?"
- "How do you currently know if people are actually reading the documentation you create?"
- "What happens during an audit — how long does it take to prove compliance?"

**Transition:** "That's really helpful context. Let me show you exactly how ARG Builder solves [specific pain they mentioned]."

---

## Part 2: Product Demonstration (20 minutes)

### Module A: AI Document Generation (4 minutes)

> "Let me start with how content gets into the system. Most teams struggle because creating documentation is painful. Watch this."

**Show:**
- Create a new document from a prompt (e.g., "Generate an incident response playbook for a 200-person SaaS company")
- AI generates structured, role-specific content in seconds
- Show the dual-persona system — same process, different views for different teams
- Highlight the template library with 525+ pre-built operational documents

**Key line:** "Your team doesn't start from a blank page. They start from an AI-generated draft that's already 80% right for your industry."

### Module B: Knowledge Graph & Discovery (4 minutes)

> "Now here's where it gets interesting. As your documentation grows, ARG Builder automatically maps the relationships between everything."

**Show:**
- Open the interactive knowledge graph
- Click on a node to show connected documents
- Demonstrate how it surfaces gaps ("You have an onboarding process but no offboarding process linked to it")
- Show the search with AI-powered suggestions

**Key line:** "This is the difference between a wiki and an intelligence layer. A wiki stores documents. ARG Builder understands how your operations connect."

### Module C: Content Governance & AI Health (4 minutes)

> "Here's the problem with every documentation tool — content goes stale. Nobody knows what's outdated until something breaks. ARG Builder solves that automatically."

**Show:**
- Content health dashboard with freshness scores
- AI-flagged stale documents (last reviewed 90+ days ago)
- Duplicate detection ("These 3 documents cover the same process — want to merge them?")
- Scheduled review reminders and approval workflows

**Key line:** "You'll never have another incident caused by someone following a 2-year-old procedure that nobody remembered to update."

### Module D: Reading Analytics & Compliance (4 minutes)

> "Let me show you something no other tool in this space does — real reading analytics."

**Show:**
- Reading heatmaps showing which sections people actually read
- Engagement scorecards per team/department
- Compliance reports ("100% of the engineering team has read the updated security policy")
- Time-to-read estimates and completion tracking

**Key line:** "For the first time, you can prove to auditors — and to your leadership — that your team isn't just storing documentation, they're actually consuming it."

### Module E: ROI Calculator (4 minutes)

> "Let me put some numbers on this for your specific situation."

**Show:**
- Open the ROI calculator
- Input their team size, average salary, estimated hours searching
- Show the calculated savings (typically 5–10x ROI)
- Compare their current tool cost vs. ARG Builder

**Key line:** "For a team of [X] people, you're looking at [$Y] in annual savings — that's a [Z]x return on your ARG Builder investment. And that's the conservative estimate."

## Part 3: Close (5 minutes)

### Pricing Presentation

> "Let me talk about what this looks like commercially."

**For teams ≤25 people:**
> "The Starter plan is $299/month flat — that includes up to 25 users, AI document generation, full-text search, and analytics. No per-seat surprises."

**For teams 25–500 people:**
> "The Professional plan is $15 per user per month — or $12/user if you commit annually. That includes everything I showed you today plus SSO, the interactive knowledge graph, and priority support."

**For 500+ people:**
> "For organizations your size, we do custom pricing starting at $2,500/month. That includes dedicated customer success, custom AI training on your data, and compliance reporting."

### Founding Customer Offer

> "We're currently onboarding our first 20 customers with a special founding program: 30% off locked for 24 months, direct Slack access to me personally, and quarterly sessions where you help shape the product roadmap. [X] spots are still available."

### Next Steps

> "Here's what I'd suggest: let's get you set up with a 14-day pilot. I'll personally configure the system with your team's actual content, and we'll do a check-in on Day 3, Day 7, and Day 14 to make sure you're seeing value. No credit card required to start. Can we get that kicked off this week?"

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We already use Notion/Confluence" | "Those are great writing tools. But they weren't built for operational governance — they can't tell you who read what, flag stale content, or map knowledge connections. ARG Builder sits on top of your existing workflow, not instead of it." |
| "Our team won't adopt another tool" | "That's exactly why we built the AI content generation and the 525+ pre-loaded templates. Your team doesn't have to create anything from scratch — they just search and find. Adoption happens because finding information becomes easier than asking a colleague." |
| "We need to involve more stakeholders" | "Absolutely. Can we schedule a 15-minute follow-up with [their boss/IT/procurement] next week? I'll prepare a one-pager with the ROI numbers specific to your team." |
| "The price is too high" | "Let me reframe that. At $15/user/month, a team of 50 costs $750/month. Your ROI calculator showed [$X] in annual savings. That's a [Y]x return. The question isn't whether you can afford ARG Builder — it's whether you can afford not to have it." |
| "We need enterprise features (SSO, compliance)" | "Those are included in Professional at $15/user. No enterprise tax. SSO, SAML, compliance reporting, approval workflows — all standard." |
| "Can we get a longer trial?" | "The 14-day pilot is designed to give you enough time to see real value without dragging out the decision. I'll be checking in on Days 3, 7, and 14 personally. If you need more time after that, we can discuss — but most teams know by Day 10." |

3. Attach the One-Pager PDF
4. Include a personalized ROI calculation screenshot
5. Propose 2 specific times for the pilot kickoff
