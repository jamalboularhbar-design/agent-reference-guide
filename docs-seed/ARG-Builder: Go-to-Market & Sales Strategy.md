# ARG-Builder: Go-to-Market & Sales Strategy

## Executive Overview

ARG-Builder's go-to-market strategy focuses on rapid market entry targeting early adopters in key verticals, followed by systematic expansion through direct sales, content marketing, and partner channels. The strategy emphasizes product-led growth, strong customer success, and vertical specialization to achieve rapid customer acquisition and high retention.

---

- 3-5 detailed case studies

- Launch content marketing program

**Success Metrics:**

**Marketing Activities:**
- Press releases and media outreach
- Content marketing (blog, whitepapers, case studies)

## Sales Strategy

### Sales Model

ARG-Builder employs a direct sales model targeting mid-market companies with a consultative, relationship-based approach. The sales process emphasizes understanding customer pain points, demonstrating value, and building long-term partnerships.

### Sales Process

**Stage 1: Prospecting & Outreach (1-2 weeks)**

Sales team identifies and reaches out to target prospects through multiple channels:
- LinkedIn outreach and connection
- Email outreach with personalized messaging
- Warm introductions from network and partners
- Inbound leads from website and content
- Referrals from existing customers

Success metric: 5-10 qualified conversations per week per sales rep.

**Stage 2: Discovery & Qualification (1-2 weeks)**

Sales rep conducts discovery call to understand prospect's situation, pain points, and needs:
- Current operational documentation practices
- Team size and structure
- Key pain points and challenges
- Budget and decision-making process
- Timeline and priorities

Success metric: 60-70% of prospects move to next stage.

**Stage 3: Demo & Value Proposition (1 week)**

Sales rep delivers personalized demo showing how ARG-Builder addresses prospect's specific pain points:
- Customized demo based on prospect's industry and use case
- ROI calculation based on prospect's specific situation
- Comparison to current approach or competitors
- Customer testimonials and case studies
- Pricing and package options

Success metric: 70-80% of prospects move to next stage.

**Stage 4: Proposal & Negotiation (1-2 weeks)**

Sales rep develops customized proposal including:
- Scope of work and implementation timeline
- Pricing and payment terms
- Success metrics and KPIs
- Support and service levels
- Contract terms

Success metric: 50-60% of prospects move to next stage.

**Stage 5: Closing (1-2 weeks)**

Sales rep works with prospect to address final questions, negotiate terms, and close the deal:
- Address final objections
- Negotiate pricing and terms
- Obtain approvals and signatures
- Schedule implementation kickoff

Success metric: 80-90% of proposals close.

**Overall Sales Cycle:** 4-8 weeks from initial contact to close.

**Win Rate Target:** 15-20% of qualified prospects.

### Sales Compensation

**Base Salary:** $80,000-$100,000 annually

**Commission Structure:**
- 10% commission on annual subscription value
- 5% commission on implementation services revenue
- Accelerators for exceeding quota (1.25x multiplier at 125% of quota, 1.5x at 150%)

**Quota:** $500,000 annual revenue per sales rep (approximately 12-15 customers per year)

**Bonus:** Annual bonus of 10-20% of base salary based on company performance and individual achievement

### Sales Enablement

**Sales Collateral:**
- Product demo video (5 minutes)
- ROI calculator
- Case studies (3-5 detailed case studies per vertical)
- Competitive positioning guide
- Pricing and package comparison
- Customer testimonials and quotes
- Industry-specific messaging
- Objection handling guide

**Sales Training:**
- Product training (features, benefits, use cases)
- Sales methodology and process
- Industry vertical training
- Competitive landscape
- Customer pain points and value proposition
- Demo and presentation skills
- Negotiation and closing skills

**Sales Tools:**
- CRM system (Salesforce or similar)
- Sales engagement platform (Outreach or similar)
- Email and calendar integration
- Call recording and analysis
- Deal tracking and forecasting
