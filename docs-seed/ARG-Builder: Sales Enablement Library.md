## Demo Script

### Pre-Demo Preparation (5 minutes)

| Task | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Review discovery notes | Personalize demo to their pain points |
| Prepare relevant persona example | Show their industry/role |
| Load their company name in demo environment | Personalization |
| Confirm attendees and their roles | Adjust depth and focus |

### Demo Flow (30 minutes)

| Segment | Duration | Content | Key Message |
|---------|----------|---------|-------------|
| Recap & Agenda | 3 min | "Based on our conversation, here's what I'll show you..." | Alignment |
| Problem Visualization | 5 min | Show the chaos (scattered docs, inconsistency) | Pain amplification |
| Solution Overview | 5 min | High-level platform walkthrough | Vision |
| Core Demo (Persona) | 8 min | Build a persona live, show AI generation | "This takes 2 weeks, not 6 months" |
| Power Features | 5 min | Search, command palette, timeline | Daily utility |
| Customer Story | 2 min | Relevant case study with metrics | Social proof |
| Q&A + Next Steps | 2 min | Address questions, propose next steps | Momentum |

### Demo Best Practices

| Practice | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| Use their language, not ours | Shows we listened in discovery |
| Show, don't tell | Let the product speak for itself |
| Pause after key moments | Let impact land |
| Ask questions during demo | Keep it conversational, not presentational |
| End with clear next step | Never end without agreed action |
