# ARG-Builder: Customer Success Playbook

## Executive Summary

This playbook defines ARG-Builder's post-onboarding customer success operations — the systems, processes, and frameworks that ensure customers achieve their desired outcomes, expand their usage, and renew with enthusiasm. Customer Success is not a support function; it is a revenue engine responsible for 120%+ net revenue retention, < 8% annual churn, and 30%+ of new ARR through expansion.

## Customer Lifecycle Management

### Post-Onboarding Journey

| Stage | Timeline | Objective | Key Activities |
|-------|----------|-----------|----------------|
| Adoption | Days 30–90 | Achieve initial value | Usage monitoring, training, quick wins |
| Value Realization | Days 90–180 | Demonstrate ROI | Success metrics review, case study development |
| Optimization | Days 180–270 | Maximize usage | Feature adoption, best practices, expansion |

## Renewal Management

### Renewal Process Timeline

| Days Before Renewal | Activity | Owner |
|--------------------|----------|-------|
| 120 days | Renewal risk assessment, health score review | CS Ops |
| 90 days | Renewal conversation initiated, ROI summary prepared | CSM |
| 60 days | Formal renewal proposal sent (pricing, terms) | CSM + Sales |
| 45 days | Negotiation (if needed), expansion discussion | CSM + Sales |
| 30 days | Contract finalized, signature requested | CSM |
| 14 days | Escalation if unsigned | VP CS |
| 0 days | Renewal effective or churn | CS Ops |

### Renewal Pricing Strategy

| Scenario | Approach | Maximum Increase |
|----------|----------|-----------------|
| Healthy + expanding | Standard renewal + expansion pricing | 5–10% uplift |
| Healthy + flat usage | Standard renewal at current rate | 3–5% CPI adjustment |
| At-risk + engaged | Renewal at current rate, add value | 0% (hold pricing) |
| At-risk + disengaged | Discount to retain, add success plan | Up to 15% discount |
| Multi-year commitment | Discount for 2–3 year term | 10–20% discount |

## Escalation Management

### Escalation Matrix

| Level | Trigger | Owner | Response Time | Resolution Target |
|-------|---------|-------|---------------|-------------------|
| L1 | Feature request, minor issue | CSM | 24 hours | 5 business days |
| L2 | Repeated issues, declining health | CSM + Manager | 4 hours | 3 business days |
| L3 | Churn risk, executive complaint | VP CS | 2 hours | 24 hours |
| L4 | Critical account, legal/contract | VP CS + CEO | 1 hour | Same day |

### Executive Escalation Protocol

When an account reaches L3 or L4:

| Step | Action | Owner | Timeline |
|------|--------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Document issue, impact, and history | CSM | Immediately |
| 2 | Brief VP CS with full context | CSM | Within 1 hour |
| 3 | VP CS contacts customer executive | VP CS | Within 4 hours |
| 4 | Create recovery plan with milestones | VP CS + CSM | Within 24 hours |
| 5 | Execute plan with daily check-ins | CSM | Ongoing |
| 6 | Post-recovery review and process improvement | VP CS | Within 1 week |
