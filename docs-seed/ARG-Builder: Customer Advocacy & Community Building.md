# ARG-Builder: Customer Advocacy & Community Building

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's customer advocacy and community building strategy — the systematic approach to transforming satisfied customers into vocal champions who drive referrals, reduce acquisition costs, and create a self-sustaining growth flywheel. Customer advocacy is the highest-leverage marketing channel for B2B SaaS.
