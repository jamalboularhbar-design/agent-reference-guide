## Case Study Production

### Case Study Framework (STAR-R)

| Section | Content | Length | Purpose |
|---------|---------|--------|---------|
| **Situation** | Company background, industry, size, challenge context | 150–200 words | Relatability |
| **Task** | Specific problem to solve, goals, requirements | 100–150 words | Problem identification |
| **Action** | How ARG-Builder was implemented, process, timeline | 200–300 words | Solution demonstration |
| **Results** | Quantified outcomes, metrics, ROI | 150–200 words | Proof of value |
| **Recommendation** | Customer quote about experience and recommendation | 50–100 words | Trust transfer |

### Case Study Interview Guide

| Question Category | Sample Questions |
|------------------|----------------|
| **Background** | "Tell me about your company and your role." / "What does your team look like?" |
| **Before** | "What was the situation before ARG-Builder?" / "What processes were you using?" / "What was the cost of the old approach?" |
| **Decision** | "Why did you choose ARG-Builder?" / "What alternatives did you consider?" / "What was the deciding factor?" |
| **Implementation** | "How was the onboarding experience?" / "How long until you saw results?" / "Any surprises during setup?" |
| **Results** | "What specific improvements have you seen?" / "Can you quantify the impact?" / "What metrics changed?" |
| **Recommendation** | "Would you recommend ARG-Builder? Why?" / "What would you tell someone considering it?" |

### Production Timeline

| Week | Activity | Owner | Deliverable |
|------|----------|-------|-------------|
| Week 1 | Customer identification + outreach | Customer Marketing | Confirmed participant |
| Week 2 | Interview (45–60 min) | Content + CSM | Recorded interview |
| Week 3 | Draft writing + data verification | Content Writer | First draft |
| Week 4 | Customer review + revisions | Customer Marketing | Approved draft |
| Week 5 | Design + formatting | Designer | Final layout |
| Week 6 | Legal approval + publish | Legal + Marketing | Live case study |

## Review Generation Program

### G2 Review Strategy

| Tactic | Target | Incentive | Timing |
|--------|--------|-----------|--------|
| Post-onboarding request | New customers (30 days) | $25 gift card | Automated email |
