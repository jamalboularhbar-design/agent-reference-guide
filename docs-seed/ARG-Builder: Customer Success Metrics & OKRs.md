# ARG-Builder: Customer Success Metrics & OKRs

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's Customer Success measurement framework — the complete system of metrics, OKRs, and performance indicators that ensure the CS team drives retention, expansion, and advocacy. Every metric connects directly to business outcomes, creating a clear line from CS activities to company revenue.

---

## CS Metrics Hierarchy

### North Star Metric

> **Net Revenue Retention (NRR) = 120%+**

This single metric captures the combined effect of retention, expansion, and contraction. It answers: "Is our existing customer base growing in value?"

---

## Tier 1: Business Outcome Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target | Measurement | Owner |
|--------|-----------|--------|-------------|-------|
| Net Revenue Retention (NRR) | (Starting MRR + Expansion - Contraction - Churn) / Starting MRR | > 120% | Monthly (trailing 12m) | VP CS |
| Gross Revenue Retention (GRR) | (Starting MRR - Contraction - Churn) / Starting MRR | > 92% | Monthly (trailing 12m) | VP CS |
| Logo Retention Rate | Customers retained / Total customers at start | > 90% | Monthly (trailing 12m) | VP CS |
| Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) | ARPU × Gross Margin / Monthly Churn Rate | > $150K | Quarterly | VP CS + Finance |
| Expansion Revenue | MRR from upsells + cross-sells + seat additions | > 20% of base MRR | Monthly | CS Team |
