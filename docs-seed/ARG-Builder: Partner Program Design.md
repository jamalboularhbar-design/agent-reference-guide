# ARG-Builder: Partner Program Design

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's complete channel partner program — a structured ecosystem of referral partners, resellers, implementation partners, and technology partners that accelerates growth while reducing customer acquisition costs. The partner channel targets 30% of new ARR by Year 3, with partner-sourced deals closing 40% faster and retaining 15% better than direct-sourced deals due to trusted relationships and implementation support.

---

## Partner Program Architecture

### Partner Types

| Type | Role | Economics | Target Partners |
|------|------|-----------|-----------------|
| Referral Partners | Introduce qualified leads | 15–20% of Year 1 ACV | Consultants, advisors, fractional COOs |
| Reseller Partners | Sell and manage customer relationship | 25–35% margin | Management consulting firms, BPO companies |
| Implementation Partners | Deliver onboarding and customization | 40–60% of services revenue | Systems integrators, boutique consultancies |
| Technology Partners | Integrate products for mutual value | Revenue share on joint deals | Slack, Notion, Salesforce, HubSpot |

### Program Tiers

| Tier | Requirements | Benefits | Target Count |
|------|-------------|----------|--------------|
| **Registered** | Sign agreement, complete training | Portal access, 15% referral fee | Unlimited |
| **Silver** | 3+ deals/year, certified team member | 20% referral/25% resell, co-marketing, leads | 50 |
| **Gold** | 10+ deals/year, 2+ certified, NPS > 8 | 25% referral/30% resell, MDF, priority support | 20 |
| **Platinum** | 25+ deals/year, dedicated team, strategic alignment | 30% referral/35% resell, exec access, joint planning | 5 |

## Partner Recruitment Strategy

### Ideal Partner Profile

| Attribute | Referral Partner | Reseller Partner | Implementation Partner |
|-----------|-----------------|------------------|----------------------|
| Industry focus | Operations, management | Technology, consulting | Systems integration |
| Customer base | 50–500 mid-market companies | 100+ enterprise accounts | 20+ active clients |
| Team size | 1–10 (boutique) | 10–100 | 5–50 |
| Geographic focus | US (initially) | US + Canada | US (initially) |
| Existing relationships | COOs, VP Ops, Chiefs of Staff | CIOs, CTOs, VP Engineering | Operations leaders |
| Revenue model | Advisory, consulting | Product sales, services | Project-based |

### Target Partner Categories

| Category | Example Companies | Why They're Ideal |
|----------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Fractional COO firms | Bolster, Chief Outsiders | Direct access to operational decision-makers |
| Management consultants | Boutique ops consultancies | Trusted advisors to mid-market CEOs |
| HR/People consultancies | Culture Amp partners, Lattice partners | Onboarding and team enablement overlap |
| Technology consultants | Salesforce partners, HubSpot partners | Already selling to our ICP |
| EOS Implementers | EOS Worldwide network | Operational framework alignment |
| Business coaches | Vistage, YPO facilitators | CEO-level relationships |

### Recruitment Outreach Sequence

**Email 1 (Introduction):**

Subject: Partnership opportunity — your clients need this

Body: Brief introduction to ARG-Builder, why their clients would benefit, and the economics (specific dollar amounts). Include one customer success metric.

**Email 2 (Value Proof — Day 5):**

Subject: How [similar partner] earned $X in referral fees last quarter

Body: Case study of a successful partner, specific deal examples, and invitation to a 15-minute overview call.

**Email 3 (Scarcity — Day 12):**

Subject: We're limiting [City/Region] partners to 5

Body: Create urgency with limited partner slots, exclusive territory benefits, and a clear CTA to schedule a call.

## Partner Enablement

### Certification Program

All partners must complete certification to ensure they represent ARG-Builder accurately and deliver quality customer experiences.

| Level | Requirements | Duration | Unlocks |
|-------|-------------|----------|---------|
| Foundations | Online course + quiz (80% pass) | 2 hours | Referral eligibility |
| Practitioner | Foundations + live workshop + demo certification | 1 day | Resell eligibility |
| Expert | Practitioner + implementation project + customer reference | 2 weeks | Implementation eligibility |
| Master | Expert + 5 successful implementations + advanced training | 3 months | Priority deal flow, speaking opportunities |

### Partner Portal Features

| Feature | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Deal registration | Register and protect opportunities |
| Lead distribution | Receive qualified leads from ARG-Builder |
| Co-branded materials | Customizable sales collateral |
| Demo environment | Sandbox for customer demonstrations |
| Training library | On-demand certification courses |
| Commission tracker | Real-time earnings and payment status |
| Support escalation | Direct access to partner support team |
| Marketing assets | Campaign templates, social content, email sequences |

### Marketing Development Fund (MDF)

Available to Gold and Platinum partners for co-marketing activities.

| Tier | Annual MDF | Eligible Activities |
|------|-----------|---------------------|
| Gold | $10K | Events, content, ads, webinars |
| Platinum | $25K | Events, content, ads, webinars, custom campaigns |

**MDF Approval Process:**
1. Partner submits activity proposal (template provided)
2. Partner Manager reviews within 5 business days
3. If approved: 50% pre-funded, 50% reimbursed on proof of execution
4. ROI report required within 30 days of activity

## Partner Performance Management

### Quarterly Partner Scorecard

| Metric | Weight | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Deals registered | 25% | Number of qualified opportunities |
| Deals closed | 30% | Revenue from partner-sourced deals |
| Customer satisfaction | 20% | NPS of partner-sourced customers |
| Certification maintenance | 15% | Team members with current certification |
| Engagement | 10% | Portal activity, event attendance, content sharing |

### Partner Health Indicators

| Status | Criteria | Action |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Thriving | Exceeding targets, high engagement, growing | Reward, expand territory, increase MDF |
| Healthy | Meeting targets, consistent engagement | Maintain, identify growth opportunities |
| At Risk | Below targets, declining engagement | Intervention: training, support, goal adjustment |
| Inactive | No deals in 90 days, no portal activity | Re-engagement campaign or offboarding |
