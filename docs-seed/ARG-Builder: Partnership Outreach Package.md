# ARG-Builder: Partnership Outreach Package

## Partnership Strategy Overview

ARG-Builder's partnership strategy targets three categories of partners: consulting firms that can recommend and implement ARG-Builder for their clients, technology companies with complementary products, and systems integrators that can embed ARG-Builder into their service offerings. The goal is to generate 30%+ of pipeline through partner channels by Year 2.

Partners agree to maintain certified team members, meet minimum referral/customer requirements, follow brand guidelines in co-marketing, provide quarterly pipeline reports, participate in partner reviews, and maintain customer satisfaction standards.

ARG-Builder agrees to provide training and certification programs, marketing materials and co-marketing support, technical support for partner-referred customers, timely commission payments, regular product updates and roadmap communication, and dedicated partner management.

## Referral Program Structure

### How Referrals Work

1. Partner identifies qualified prospect and submits referral through partner portal
2. ARG-Builder sales team engages prospect within 24 hours
3. Partner receives status updates throughout sales process
4. Upon close, partner receives commission on Year 1 subscription value
5. Commission is paid monthly as customer pays subscription

### Referral Qualification Criteria

A qualified referral meets the following criteria: company has 200+ employees, identified operational documentation need, budget authority confirmed, decision timeline within 6 months, and contact information for decision maker provided.

### Commission Structure

| Scenario | Commission |
|----------|-----------|
| Qualified referral → closed deal | 15–30% of Year 1 subscription |
| Referral + partner assists in sales | Additional 5% bonus |
| Referral → Enterprise tier | Additional 5% bonus |
| Renewal commission (Year 2+) | 10% of renewal value |

## Partner Enablement

### Certification Program

**Level 1: ARG-Builder Fundamentals (4 hours)**

Covers platform overview, value proposition, customer use cases, and basic demonstration skills. Required for all partner team members within 90 days of partnership signing.

**Level 2: ARG-Builder Implementation (8 hours)**

Covers implementation methodology, discovery techniques, customization, training delivery, and customer success. Required for team members delivering implementations.

**Level 3: ARG-Builder Expert (16 hours)**

Covers advanced customization, integration development, complex implementations, and strategic consulting. Optional for senior consultants and practice leaders.

### Partner Portal Features

The partner portal provides deal registration, pipeline tracking, commission reporting, marketing materials library, training and certification access, support ticket submission, product updates and roadmap, and co-marketing request forms.

### Marketing Development Fund (MDF)

Gold and Platinum partners receive quarterly MDF allocation for co-marketing activities:

| Tier | Quarterly MDF | Eligible Activities |
|------|---------------|---------------------|
| Gold | $5,000 | Webinars, events, content, advertising |
| Platinum | $15,000 | All Gold activities + custom campaigns, events |

MDF requires pre-approval and post-activity reporting demonstrating lead generation results.
