# Riad & Routes — Tour Booking & Confirmation Workflow

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the end-to-end workflow for booking, confirming, and managing day tours and multi-day excursions offered through Riad & Routes. Tours are a significant revenue stream (averaging 35% of total guest spend) and a key differentiator from competitors. The process must be seamless, personalized, and error-free.

## 3. Booking Workflow

### 3.1 Step 1: Guest Inquiry (Day 0)

Guest expresses interest via:
- In-person conversation with Guest Relations
- WhatsApp message to concierge number
- Email to experiences@riadandroutes.com
- Booking widget on property tablet in the lounge

**Response SLA:** Acknowledge within 15 minutes during operating hours (08:00–22:00). After hours, auto-reply confirms receipt and promises response by 08:30 next day.

### 3.2 Step 2: Needs Assessment (Within 30 minutes of inquiry)

The Tour Coordinator asks qualifying questions:

1. Which dates are you considering?
2. How many people in your group? Any children?
3. Any physical limitations or accessibility needs?
4. Preferred pace: relaxed/moderate/active?
5. Any specific interests (photography, food, history, adventure)?
6. Budget range (if comfortable sharing)?

Based on answers, the coordinator recommends 2-3 suitable options with brief descriptions.

### 3.3 Step 3: Proposal & Quotation (Within 2 hours)

Once the guest selects a preferred tour, the coordinator prepares a formal quotation:

- Tour name and date
- Pickup time and location
- Included services (transport, guide, meals, entrance fees)
- Excluded items (personal purchases, tips, optional activities)
- Total price with applicable surcharges/discounts
- Cancellation policy summary
- Payment terms (50% deposit to confirm, balance on day of tour)

The quotation is sent via WhatsApp or email as a branded PDF.

### 3.4 Step 4: Confirmation & Deposit (Guest action required)

Guest confirms by:
- Replying "Confirmed" to the WhatsApp message, OR
- Signing the printed quotation at the front desk

Payment options for deposit:
- Cash (MAD, EUR, USD accepted at daily rate)
- Card payment at front desk (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Bank transfer (for multi-day tours only, must clear 48h before departure)

### 3.5 Step 5: Supplier Coordination (Within 4 hours of confirmation)

The Tour Coordinator contacts all suppliers:

| Supplier Type | Communication Method | Confirmation Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Guide (licensed) | WhatsApp + calendar invite | Written confirmation + guide name |
| Driver | WhatsApp group + dispatch system | Vehicle assignment + plate number |
| Restaurant (if lunch included) | Phone call + email | Menu confirmation + dietary notes |
| Activity provider (balloon, cooking class) | Email with booking reference | Booking reference number |
| Accommodation (multi-day) | Email + phone | Room confirmation + special requests |

All supplier confirmations must be received and logged in the booking system within 24 hours. If a supplier cannot confirm, the coordinator must find an alternative and notify the guest of any changes.

### 3.6 Step 6: Pre-Tour Briefing (T-12 hours)

The evening before the tour, Guest Relations delivers a briefing card to the guest's room during turndown:

- Pickup time and meeting point
- What to bring (sunscreen, comfortable shoes, camera, cash for tips)
- Weather forecast for the day
- Guide name and photo
- Emergency contact number

For early-morning tours (before 07:00), a breakfast box is arranged with the kitchen.

### 3.7 Step 7: Day-of Execution

- Driver arrives at property 15 minutes before scheduled pickup
- Guest Relations confirms guest is ready and introduces the guide
- Guide carries a branded welcome pack (water, snacks, wet wipes, property business card)
- Tour Coordinator monitors via WhatsApp group (guide sends photo updates at key stops)
- Any issues during the tour are escalated to the coordinator immediately

### 3.8 Step 8: Post-Tour Follow-up (Within 2 hours of return)

- Guest Relations checks in with the guest: "How was your day? Is there anything else we can arrange?"
- Tour Coordinator collects guide's debrief notes (any issues, guest feedback, suggestions)
- Balance payment is collected if not already paid
- Guest is asked to rate the experience (1-5 stars) — logged in CRM

## 4. Cancellation & Refund Policy

| Cancellation Window | Refund |
| :--- | :--- |
| 72+ hours before departure | Full refund of deposit |
| 24-72 hours before departure | 50% of deposit refunded |
| Less than 24 hours | No refund (unless weather/safety cancellation) |
| Weather/safety cancellation by R&R | Full refund or reschedule at guest's choice |
| No-show | No refund |
