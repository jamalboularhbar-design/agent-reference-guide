## Slide 2: The Problem

**Mid-market companies are bleeding efficiency.**

American mid-market companies (200–1,000 employees) face a compounding operational crisis. Institutional knowledge lives in people's heads, not systems. When key employees leave, critical processes disappear with them. Onboarding new hires takes 6–12 months to reach full productivity. Teams across departments operate with inconsistent standards, creating quality variance and client dissatisfaction.

The cost of this problem is staggering:

| Pain Point | Annual Cost per Company |
|------------|------------------------|
| Knowledge loss from turnover | $1.2M in lost productivity |
| Extended onboarding cycles | $840K in delayed value creation |
| Operational inconsistency | $600K in rework and quality failures |
| Failed process documentation projects | $200K in wasted consulting fees |
| **Total annual cost** | **$2.8M per mid-market company** |

Traditional solutions — consulting firms, wiki tools, manual documentation — fail because they require months of effort, produce static documents that decay immediately, and never achieve team adoption.

## Slide 3: The Solution

**ARG-Builder: Autonomous Operational Intelligence**

ARG-Builder is an AI-powered platform that autonomously generates professional operational reference guides in 12–19 hours. The platform interviews stakeholders, maps processes, defines personas, and produces an interactive, searchable, team-ready system — complete with advanced search, command palette, process timelines, and PDF export.

**How it works:**

1. **Input:** Business owner describes their operations, personas, and processes in natural language
2. **Generation:** AI agent autonomously designs, builds, and refines a complete operational reference guide
3. **Delivery:** Team receives an interactive, searchable platform with all processes documented to professional standards
4. **Adoption:** Built-in features (search, command palette, timelines) drive 92% team adoption within 2 weeks

**What makes it different:**

Unlike static wikis or expensive consulting engagements, ARG-Builder produces a living system that teams actually use. The guides are interactive, searchable, and designed with the same attention to craft as a premium software product.
