# Market Research Notes

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors (Knowledge Management / Operational Guides)
| Company | Pricing | Target | Positioning |
|---------|---------|--------|-------------|
| Guru | $15-25/seat/mo, Enterprise custom | Mid-market to Enterprise | AI-powered knowledge layer, verified source of truth |
| Trainual | $249/mo flat (10 seats), $3-5/user/mo | SMB to Mid-market | Employee training & onboarding playbooks |
| Scribe | $15/seat/mo, Enterprise $39/user + $1300/mo | All segments | Auto-document processes (screen capture) |
| Notion | $10-15/seat/mo | All segments | All-in-one workspace (broad, not specialized) |
| Tettra | ~$10/user/mo | SMB | Lightweight internal knowledge base |
| Confluence | $5.75-11/user/mo | Mid-market to Enterprise | Wiki-style documentation (Atlassian ecosystem) |
| Document360 | $149-599/mo | Mid-market | Knowledge base with analytics |
| Slite | $8-12/user/mo | SMB to Mid-market | Team knowledge base with AI |

## Target Buyer Personas

### Primary: VP/Director of Operations (Mid-Market 200-2000 employees)
- Pain: Scattered SOPs across Google Docs, Notion, Confluence
- Pain: New hires take 3-6 months to find relevant processes
- Pain: No visibility into which docs are stale/outdated
- Budget: $5K-50K/year for operational tools
- Decision: Usually owns budget, may need CFO sign-off above $25K

### Secondary: HR Director / Head of People
- Pain: Onboarding takes too long, inconsistent across teams
- Pain: Can't track if new hires actually read required materials
- Pain: No way to measure knowledge retention
- Budget: Part of L&D budget, $10K-100K/year
- Decision: Owns budget for training tools

### Tertiary: CTO / VP Engineering (SaaS Builder Teams)
- Pain: Engineering playbooks scattered, tribal knowledge
- Pain: Incident response docs outdated
- Pain: No single source of truth for architectural decisions
- Budget: Engineering tools budget, $10K-50K/year

## Enterprise Pain Points (from research)
1. Knowledge scattered across 5+ tools
2. No governance over AI-generated content
3. Outdated documentation with no review cycles
4. No analytics on knowledge consumption
5. Onboarding time too long (avg 6 months to full productivity)
