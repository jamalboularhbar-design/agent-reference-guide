# ARG Builder — AI-Powered Operational Intelligence

> **Your team's knowledge, always within reach.**

## The Problem

Mid-market companies (200–2,000 employees) lose an average of **$4.3 million per year** in productivity because their operational knowledge is scattered across disconnected tools [1]. Employees spend **9.3 hours per week** searching for information they already have [2], and **67% cannot find what they need** when they need it [3]. Legacy tools like Notion, Confluence, and Google Docs were built for writing — not for governing operational knowledge at scale.

## The Solution

ARG Builder is the **AI-powered operational knowledge platform** that turns scattered SOPs, playbooks, and guides into a living, searchable intelligence layer your entire team can trust. Unlike generic wikis, ARG Builder is purpose-built for operational governance — with AI content health monitoring, reading analytics, and enterprise-grade version control.

## Key Capabilities

| Capability | What It Does | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| **Knowledge Graph** | Visualizes how all documents connect | Find gaps and orphaned content instantly |
| **AI Content Governance** | Detects duplicates, flags stale docs, scans broken links | Knowledge stays healthy without manual audits |
| **Reading Analytics** | Tracks who reads what, when, and how deeply | Prove compliance, measure adoption, identify gaps |
| **Version Control** | Full diff view, rollback, and audit trail | Never lose a change, always know who changed what |
| **Approval Workflows** | Multi-stage review with scheduled publishing | Ensure quality before content goes live |
| **Dual Persona System** | Role-based operational views | Different teams see different workflows seamlessly |
