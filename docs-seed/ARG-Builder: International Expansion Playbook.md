# ARG-Builder: International Expansion Playbook

## Executive Summary

This playbook defines ARG-Builder's strategy for expanding beyond the US market into international territories. The expansion targets $5M+ in international ARR by Year 4, representing 20% of total revenue. The approach is methodical: validate demand in English-speaking markets first (UK, Canada, Australia), then expand to Western Europe (Germany, France, Netherlands), and finally address high-growth markets (UAE, Singapore, Japan). Each market entry follows a structured playbook that minimizes risk while maximizing speed to revenue.

## Phase 2: Western Europe (Months 18–30)

### Germany

Germany represents the largest European mid-market opportunity but requires significant localization investment due to language requirements and strict data privacy culture.

| Dimension | Strategy |
|-----------|----------|
| Entity | GmbH (required for enterprise sales) |
| Data residency | AWS eu-central-1 (Frankfurt) — mandatory for German enterprises |
| Pricing | EUR pricing, annual contracts preferred (German buying behavior) |
| Sales | German-speaking AE (Frankfurt or Munich based) |
| Marketing | German-language content, DMEXCO, Bits & Pretzels |
