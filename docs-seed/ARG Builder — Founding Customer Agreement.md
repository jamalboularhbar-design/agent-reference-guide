## Founding Customer Program Overview

This agreement outlines the terms of the ARG Builder Founding Customer Program between **ARG Builder** ("Provider") and **[Company Name]** ("Customer"). The Founding Customer Program is a limited partnership designed to give early adopters significant pricing advantages and product influence in exchange for feedback, case study participation, and early commitment.

---

## 1. Pricing Terms

### 1.1 Founding Customer Discount

Customer receives a **30% discount** on their selected plan, locked for **24 months** from the Effective Date. This discount applies to the base subscription price and is not combinable with other promotions.

| Plan | Standard Price | Founding Customer Price | Savings (24 months) |
|------|---------------|----------------------|---------------------|
| Starter | $299/month | $209/month | $2,160 |
| Professional (per user) | $15/user/month | $10.50/user/month | $108/user |
| Enterprise | Custom | 30% off negotiated rate | Varies |

### 1.2 Price Lock Guarantee

The Founding Customer price is guaranteed for 24 months regardless of any general price increases during that period. After 24 months, Customer transitions to the then-current standard pricing with 60 days advance written notice.

### 1.3 Payment Terms

Payment is due monthly via credit card or ACH. Annual prepayment is available at an additional 10% discount (total 40% off standard pricing for annual founding customers).

## 2. What Customer Receives

### 2.1 Product Access

- Full access to all features in the selected plan tier
- Early access to new features before general availability (minimum 2 weeks advance)
- Priority bug fixes (24-hour response time for critical issues)

### 2.2 Founder Access

- Direct Slack channel with Jamal Boularhbar (Founder & CEO)
- Response time guarantee: within 4 business hours
- Monthly 30-minute strategy call (optional, scheduled at Customer's convenience)

### 2.3 Product Influence

- Quarterly product roadmap input sessions (60 minutes, virtual)
- Priority feature request queue (founding customer requests evaluated first)
- Advance notice of any breaking changes (minimum 30 days)
- Invitation to annual Founding Customer Advisory Board

### 2.4 Implementation Support

- Complimentary Standard Implementation ($5,000 value) for Professional and Enterprise tiers
- Includes: AI-powered operational audit, system configuration, content migration assistance, and team onboarding
- Timeline: 1–2 weeks from agreement signing

---

## 3. What Customer Provides

### 3.1 Feedback Commitment

- Participate in one 30-minute feedback session per quarter (4 per year)
- Respond to product surveys within 5 business days (estimated 2–3 per quarter)
- Report bugs and usability issues through the designated channel

### 3.2 Case Study Participation

- Agree to participate in one written case study within the first 6 months
- Case study will include: challenge, solution, measurable results
- Customer retains approval rights over all published content
- Customer may opt for anonymous case study if preferred

### 3.3 Reference Availability

- Agree to serve as a reference for up to 3 prospective customers per year
- Reference calls limited to 15 minutes each
- Customer may decline any specific reference request without penalty

### 3.4 Minimum Commitment

- 12-month minimum subscription commitment
- Early termination permitted with 60 days written notice (discount forfeited for remaining months)

## 5. Data & Privacy

- Customer retains full ownership of all data uploaded to the platform
- Provider will not access Customer data without explicit permission (except for technical support when requested)
- Data export available at any time in standard formats (JSON, CSV, PDF)
- Upon termination, Customer data is retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted
- Provider complies with applicable data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA)

---

## 6. Termination

### 6.1 By Customer

Customer may terminate with 60 days written notice. If terminated before the 12-month minimum commitment, the founding discount is forfeited for the remaining commitment period (standard pricing applies for any remaining months).

### 6.2 By Provider

Provider may terminate with 90 days written notice. In this case, Customer receives a pro-rated refund for any prepaid period and retains data export rights for 60 days.

### 6.3 Post-Termination

- All Customer data available for export for 30 days post-termination
- Case study and reference obligations cease immediately upon termination
- Any published case studies remain live unless Customer requests removal
