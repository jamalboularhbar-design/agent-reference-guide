# ARG Builder Pricing Research Notes

## Market Benchmarks (May 2026)

### Competitor Pricing (Knowledge Management / AI Operations)
- **Confluence**: $5.42/user/month (Standard), $10.44/user/month (Premium)
  - 100 users = $542–$1,044/month; 500 users = $2,710–$5,220/month
- **Guru**: $250/month minimum (10-seat min), ~$25/user/month
- **Notion**: $20/user/month (Business tier with AI)
- **Tettra**: $8.33–$12/user/month
- **Slite**: $12.50/user/month (Premium)
- **Bloomfire**: ~$25/user/month ($1,250/month min for 50 users)
- **AI-native platforms**: $20,000+/year for mid-market

### B2B SaaS ACV Benchmarks
- SMB-focused SaaS: $5,000–$15,000 ACV
- Mid-market SaaS: $15,000–$50,000 ACV
- Median ACV across all private SaaS: $26,265 (2025)
- Mid-sized companies spend 4-8% of revenue on SaaS

### AI-Specific Pricing Trends (Bessemer 2026 Playbook)
1. **Hybrid models win**: Base subscription + usage/outcome tiers
2. **Per-seat is dying for AI**: AI-native companies abandoning seat-based pricing
3. **Three models**: Copilots (per-seat), Agents (outcome-based), AI-enabled Services (workflow-based)
4. **AI gross margins**: 50-60% vs 80-90% for traditional SaaS
5. **Key principle**: "Pricing ties directly to value delivered, not access granted"
6. **Outcome-based example**: Intercom charges $0.99 per resolved ticket
7. **Sweet spot test**: Raise price until customer says "we have to think about that"

### Stripe AI Pricing Insights
- Usage-based = default because it feels fair
- Hybrid = platform fee + variable for compute-heavy features
- Credits/outcomes = prepaid credits or per-result pricing
- Tiers help with customer segmentation

## ARG Builder Positioning Analysis

### What ARG Builder Actually Sells
- NOT just a knowledge base (that's commodity at $5-25/user/month)
- AI-powered operational intelligence platform
- Document generation, knowledge graphs, workflow automation
- Multi-vertical: Hospitality, Healthcare, Professional Services, Retail, Manufacturing, SaaS
- Vision: Build custom AI-powered solutions for each vertical

### Competitive Moat
- AI document generation (not just storage)
- Knowledge graph visualization (unique differentiator)
- Workflow automation with AI
- Multi-vertical platform play (not single-vertical)
- Implementation services (high-touch onboarding)

### Target Customer Profile
- Mid-market: 200–3,000 employees
- Revenue: $50M–$500M
- Current pain: Scattered operational knowledge, manual processes
- Budget: IT/operations budget, not just "SaaS tools" line item
