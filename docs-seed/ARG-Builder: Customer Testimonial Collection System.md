# ARG-Builder: Customer Testimonial Collection System

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's systematic approach to collecting, managing, and deploying customer testimonials — the social proof engine that accelerates trust-building and shortens sales cycles. Testimonials are the most credible form of marketing; a structured system ensures a continuous pipeline of fresh, relevant, and compelling customer voices.

## Collection Process

### Request Templates

| Stage | Channel | Template |
|-------|---------|----------|
| Initial ask | Email (CSM) | "Hi [Name], I noticed [specific achievement]. Would you be open to sharing a brief quote about your experience? It takes < 5 minutes and helps other [industry] leaders discover what's possible." |
| Follow-up (no response) | Email (Day 3) | "Just circling back — I'd love to feature [Company] as a success story. Happy to make it as easy as possible. Would a 2-minute phone call work?" |
| Video request | Email + calendar link | "We'd love to capture your story on video. It's a 15-minute conversation (we handle all editing). Here's a calendar link to schedule at your convenience." |
| Case study request | Email + brief | "We'd like to develop a detailed case study featuring [Company]. Here's a brief outline — it's a 30-minute interview and we write everything. You just approve." |
| Review request | Email + direct link | "Would you mind sharing your experience on G2? Here's a direct link — takes about 3 minutes. Your honest feedback helps other teams make informed decisions." |

### Interview Guide (Case Studies)

| Section | Questions | Duration |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Background | "Tell me about your role and team." "What was the situation before ARG-Builder?" | 5 min |
| Challenge | "What specific problem were you trying to solve?" "What was the impact on your team?" | 5 min |
| Solution | "Why did you choose ARG-Builder?" "How was the implementation?" | 5 min |
| Results | "What results have you seen?" "Can you quantify the impact?" | 10 min |
| Recommendation | "What would you tell someone considering ARG-Builder?" | 5 min |

## Testimonial Library Management

### Organization System

| Category | Tags | Purpose |
|----------|------|---------|
| Industry | SaaS, Healthcare, Finance, Manufacturing, etc. | Industry-specific selling |
| Company size | SMB, Mid-Market, Enterprise | Segment-specific proof |
| Use case | Onboarding, Knowledge Management, Process Standardization | Feature-specific proof |
| Persona | VP Ops, Knowledge Manager, CEO, HR Director | Persona-specific selling |
| Outcome | Time saved, revenue impact, adoption rate, satisfaction | Outcome-specific proof |
| Format | Video, written, quote, review, logo | Channel-appropriate selection |
| Recency | Current quarter, last 6 months, last year | Freshness management |

### Freshness Policy

| Age | Status | Action Required |
|-----|--------|----------------|
| 0–6 months | Fresh | Active use across all channels |
| 6–12 months | Current | Continue using, plan refresh |
| 12–18 months | Aging | Seek updated quote or new testimonial |
| 18+ months | Stale | Archive, replace with fresh content |
