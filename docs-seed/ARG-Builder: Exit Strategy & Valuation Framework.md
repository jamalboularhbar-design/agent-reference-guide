## Exit Timing Analysis

### Optimal Exit Windows

| Window | Conditions | Rationale |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Year 3–4 (Early) | $8–15M ARR, 150%+ growth, strong NRR | Acquirer pays for growth trajectory, not current revenue |
| Year 5–6 (Optimal) | $20–35M ARR, 80%+ growth, profitable | Maximum multiple × meaningful revenue = highest absolute value |
| Year 7+ (Late) | $50M+ ARR, market leadership | IPO-ready or premium strategic acquisition |

### Exit Readiness Indicators

| Indicator | Ready | Not Ready |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Revenue predictability | < 10% variance from forecast | > 20% variance |
| Customer concentration | No customer > 5% of revenue | Top customer > 15% |
| Team depth | 2-deep in every critical role | Key-person dependencies |
| Documentation | All processes documented | Tribal knowledge |
| Legal cleanliness | Clean cap table, IP assigned, no litigation | Unresolved issues |
| Financial audit | Audited financials (2+ years) | Unaudited |
| Growth trajectory | Consistent or accelerating | Decelerating |
| Market position | Clear category leader or strong #2 | Undifferentiated |

| Section | Contents |
