## Exercise & Tax Considerations

### Option Types

| Type | Tax Treatment | Best For | 409A Requirement |
|------|-------------|----------|-----------------|
| ISO (Incentive Stock Options) | Favorable (LTCG if held 1yr + 2yr) | US employees (up to $100K/year vesting) | Yes |
| NSO (Non-Qualified Stock Options) | Ordinary income on exercise spread | Contractors, advisors, excess over $100K | Yes |
| RSU (Restricted Stock Units) | Ordinary income on vest date | Later-stage, public companies | N/A |
| Early Exercise (83(b)) | Ordinary income on grant (minimal if early) | Very early employees, low FMV | Within 30 days |

### 409A Valuation Schedule

| Trigger | Action Required | Timeline |
|---------|----------------|----------|
| Initial grant | First 409A valuation | Before any grants |
| Every 12 months | Annual refresh | Within 12 months of prior |
| Material event | New valuation required | Within 30 days |
| Fundraising round | New valuation (often = round price discount) | At close |
