## Hiring Process

### Standard Hiring Pipeline

| Stage | Duration | Activities | Decision Maker | Pass Rate |
|-------|----------|-----------|---------------|-----------|
| 1. Application/Sourcing | — | Resume review, LinkedIn screen | Recruiter | 20% |
| 2. Recruiter screen | 30 min | Culture fit, logistics, motivation | Recruiter | 50% |
| 3. Hiring manager screen | 45 min | Role fit, experience deep-dive | Hiring Manager | 50% |
| 4. Technical/Skills assessment | 60–90 min | Role-specific evaluation | Interviewer panel | 40% |
| 5. Culture & values interview | 45 min | Values alignment, collaboration style | Cross-functional | 70% |
| 6. Final/Executive interview | 30 min | Vision alignment, questions | CEO/VP | 80% |
| 7. Offer | 1–3 days | Package, negotiation, close | Hiring Manager + People | 85% |

**Overall funnel:** 100 applicants → 20 screened → 10 HM screen → 5 assessed → 3 culture → 2 final → 1.5 offers → 1 hire

### Interview Scorecards

| Competency | Weight | Scoring (1–5) | Evidence Required |
|-----------|--------|---------------|------------------|
| Technical/functional skills | 30% | Demonstrated ability in assessment | Specific examples, portfolio, test results |
| Problem-solving | 20% | Structured thinking, creativity | How they approach novel problems |
| Communication | 15% | Clarity, listening, writing | Interview quality, written samples |
| Culture alignment | 15% | Values match, collaboration | Behavioral examples, references |
| Growth potential | 10% | Learning agility, ambition | Career trajectory, self-awareness |
| Domain expertise | 10% | Industry knowledge, network | Relevant experience, insights |

### DEI Practices in Hiring

|----------|---------------|--------|
| Blind resume review | Remove names, schools, photos | Reduces unconscious bias |
| Structured interviews | Same questions, same order, scored rubric | Consistent evaluation |

## Retention & Engagement

### Retention Levers

| Lever | Strategy | Measurement |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Career growth | Clear levels, promotion criteria, stretch assignments | Promotion rate, internal mobility |
| Compensation | Annual market adjustment, equity refresh, performance bonus | Comp ratio, offer competitiveness |
| Manager quality | Manager training, 360 feedback, skip-levels | Manager NPS, team retention |
| Mission connection | All-hands updates, customer stories, impact metrics | Engagement survey scores |
| Work-life balance | Flexible hours, async culture, PTO enforcement | Burnout indicators, PTO usage |
| Recognition | Peer recognition program, spot bonuses, public celebration | Recognition frequency |

### Early Warning Indicators (Flight Risk)

| Signal | Detection Method | Intervention |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|
| Reduced engagement (meetings, Slack) | Activity monitoring | Manager 1:1, check-in |
| Negative sentiment in surveys | Pulse survey analysis | Skip-level, address concerns |
| LinkedIn profile update | Recruiter alert tools | Retention conversation |
| Peer departure | Team churn tracking | Proactive stay interview |
| Passed over for promotion | Promotion cycle review | Development plan, timeline |
| Life event (relocation, family) | Manager awareness | Flexibility discussion |
