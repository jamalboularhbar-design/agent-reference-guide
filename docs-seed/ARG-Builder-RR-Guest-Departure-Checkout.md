# Riad & Routes — Guest Departure & Checkout Process

## 1. Purpose

The departure experience is the last impression a guest carries with them — and the impression they share in reviews. This SOP ensures every checkout is smooth, warm, and leaves guests feeling valued, with clear processes for billing, feedback collection, luggage handling, and post-departure follow-up.

## 2. Pre-Departure Preparation (Evening Before)

### 2.1 Night Before Actions (by 21:00)

| Task | Responsible | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Review guest folio | Night Auditor | Verify all charges are posted, no pending items |
| Prepare final bill | Night Auditor | Print and slip under door by 22:00 (or email if preferred) |
| Confirm transfer | Front Desk | Reconfirm airport/station transfer time with driver |
| Pack departure gift | Housekeeping | Prepare branded gift bag (see Section 6) |
| Note special requests | Front Desk | Early breakfast? Luggage storage? Late checkout? |
| Flag VIP departures | Guest Relations | GM to be present for VIP farewells |

### 2.2 Guest Communication (Evening Before)

WhatsApp message sent at 19:00:
> Bonsoir [Name],
>
> As your stay with us comes to an end tomorrow, I wanted to confirm a few details:
>
> 🕐 Checkout time: 11:00 (or [agreed late checkout time])
> 🚗 Transfer: [Confirmed at HH:MM / Not arranged — would you like us to book one?]
> 🍳 Breakfast: Available from 07:30 as usual
>
> Your bill has been prepared and [slipped under your door / sent to your email]. Please let us know if you have any questions.
>
> We've truly enjoyed hosting you. See you tomorrow morning! ☀️

## 3. Checkout Morning Workflow

### 3.1 Timeline

| Time (relative to checkout) | Action | Who |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| -2 hours | Confirm driver is en route (if transfer booked) | Front Desk |
| -1.5 hours | Ensure breakfast is served promptly if guest is eating | F&B team |
| -1 hour | Prepare reception for checkout (bill ready, card terminal, receipt paper) | Front Desk |
| -30 minutes | Offer luggage assistance ("May I help bring your bags down?") | Porter |
| -15 minutes | Final room check by housekeeping (lost items, minibar, damage) | Housekeeping |
| Checkout time | Guest arrives at reception for settlement | Front Desk |
| +5 minutes | Farewell, gift presentation, photo offer | Guest Relations / GM |
| +10 minutes | Guest departs, wave goodbye from door | All available staff |

### 3.2 Settlement Process

| Payment Method | Process | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Credit card (pre-authorized) | Charge final amount to card on file, provide receipt | Most common for direct bookings |
| Credit card (new) | Process payment on terminal, provide receipt | If guest prefers different card |
| Cash (MAD) | Count in front of guest, provide receipt | Offer to break large notes |
| Cash (EUR/USD) | Accept at posted exchange rate, provide receipt in MAD equivalent | Rate displayed at reception |
| Bank transfer | Provide bank details, mark as "pending" until received | For long stays or corporate |
| OTA prepaid (Booking.com, Airbnb) | No payment needed at checkout, confirm extras only | Extras charged to card on file |

### 3.3 Bill Dispute Handling

If a guest questions a charge:
1. Listen fully without interrupting
2. Pull up the detailed folio and explain each charge calmly
3. If the charge is legitimate: explain clearly with supporting evidence (signed chit, booking confirmation)
4. If there's any ambiguity: remove the charge immediately ("I'd rather you leave happy than debate 200 MAD")
5. Never argue. The cost of a disputed charge is always less than the cost of a bad review.

## 4. Feedback Collection

### 4.1 In-Person (At Checkout)

The Front Desk Manager or Guest Relations asks:
- "How was your stay overall? Is there anything we could have done better?"
- Listen actively, take notes, thank them for the feedback
- If they mention something negative: apologize sincerely, note it for follow-up
- If they're enthusiastic: "We'd love if you could share that on TripAdvisor — it really helps us!"

### 4.2 Digital Feedback (Post-Departure)

| Timing | Channel | Content |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| T+1 day | WhatsApp | Thank you + review request (see WhatsApp SOP) |
| T+3 days | Email | Personalized thank-you + feedback survey link + review links |
| T+7 days | Email | "We miss you" + return offer (10% off next stay, direct booking only) |

### 4.3 Feedback Processing

All feedback (verbal and written) is logged in the Guest Feedback Register:
- Date, guest name, room, length of stay
- Positive comments (verbatim quotes)
- Negative comments (verbatim quotes)
- Action taken / to be taken
- Responsible person and deadline

Monthly review by GM — patterns identified, structural improvements implemented.

## 5. Luggage & Transport

### 5.1 Luggage Handling

| Scenario | Protocol |
| :--- | :--- |
| Standard departure | Porter collects bags from room 30 min before checkout, stores at reception |
| Late flight (luggage storage) | Bags tagged and stored in locked luggage room, guest given claim tag |
| Luggage storage duration | Maximum 24 hours (longer requires advance arrangement) |
| Fragile/valuable items | Guest carries personally, or stored in office safe with signed receipt |
| Shipping luggage (international) | Arrange via DHL/FedEx, guest pays, we handle logistics |

### 5.2 Transfer Coordination

| Transfer Type | Lead Time | Cost | Includes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Airport (Menara) | 1 hour before flight | 350 MAD | A/C vehicle, bottled water, driver meets at riad door |
| Train station | 30 min before departure | 200 MAD | Same as above |
| Bus station (CTM/Supratours) | 30 min before departure | 200 MAD | Same as above |
| Onward hotel (within Marrakech) | 15 min before checkout | 150 MAD | Door-to-door |
| Day trip departure (Atlas, Essaouira) | Per tour schedule | Included in tour price | Driver briefed on itinerary |

### 5.3 Lost Item Protocol

If a guest reports a lost item after departure:
1. Check the room immediately (housekeeping searches thoroughly)
2. Check lost & found register (items found during cleaning are logged with photo)
3. If found: contact guest within 2 hours, arrange shipping (guest pays courier cost)
4. If not found: inform guest honestly, check CCTV if theft suspected
5. All found items stored for 90 days, then donated to charity

## 6. Departure Gift

Every departing guest receives a branded gift bag containing:

| Item | Cost | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Small bottle of argan oil (50ml) | 35 MAD | Signature Moroccan souvenir |
| Handwritten thank-you card (by GM or concierge) | 5 MAD | Personal touch, memorable |
| Branded bookmark or luggage tag | 10 MAD | Subtle brand reminder |
| Business card with QR code (TripAdvisor) | 2 MAD | Review generation |
| 2 Moroccan pastries (wrapped) | 15 MAD | Sweet farewell for the journey |

**Total cost per gift bag: ~67 MAD** (justified by review generation and return booking value)

**VIP guests receive an upgraded gift:**
- Full-size argan oil (100ml) + rose water
- Handmade ceramic coaster (from artisan partner)
- Personalized photo from their stay (printed, in branded frame)

## 7. Post-Departure Internal Tasks

| Task | Deadline | Responsible |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Room inspection (damage, missing items) | Within 1 hour of departure | Housekeeping Supervisor |
| Update guest profile in PMS (preferences noted for return) | Same day | Front Desk |
| Log any maintenance issues found | Same day | Housekeeping → Maintenance |
| Process any pending charges | Same day | Finance |
| Send post-departure WhatsApp | T+1 day | Guest Relations |
| Update CRM with stay notes | Within 48 hours | Guest Relations |
| If returning guest: flag for recognition on next visit | Immediately | Front Desk |

## 8. Late Checkout & Extensions

| Request | Policy | Charge |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Late checkout until 13:00 | Granted freely if room not needed | Complimentary |
| Late checkout until 15:00 | Subject to availability | +30% of nightly rate |
| Late checkout until 18:00 | Subject to availability | +50% of nightly rate |
| Extension by 1+ nights | Check availability, adjust rate if needed | Published rate for dates |
| Day-use after checkout | Use of pool/lounge until 17:00 | 500 MAD (includes lunch) |

| KPI | Target | Measurement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| Bill dispute rate | < 3% of checkouts | Monthly tracking |
| Post-departure review conversion | > 25% of guests leave a review | Monthly tracking |
| Lost item recovery rate | > 90% (items found and returned) | Quarterly audit |
| Return booking rate (within 24 months) | > 15% | CRM tracking |
