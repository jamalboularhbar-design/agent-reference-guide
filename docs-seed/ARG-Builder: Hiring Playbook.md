# ARG-Builder: Hiring Playbook

## Executive Summary

This playbook defines the complete hiring strategy for ARG-Builder's first 12 months — scaling from 3 founders to a 25-person team. It includes job descriptions, interview scorecards, compensation benchmarks, recruitment channels, and a structured hiring timeline aligned with business milestones. Every hire is mapped to a specific revenue or product milestone, ensuring the team scales in lockstep with business growth.

---

## Hiring Philosophy

ARG-Builder's hiring philosophy is built on four principles: hire for slope over intercept (growth potential over current credentials), prioritize builders over managers in the first 18 months, optimize for culture-add rather than culture-fit, and never compromise on intellectual honesty. Every team member should be comfortable operating in ambiguity, moving fast, and owning outcomes rather than activities.

## Critical Hire #1: VP Sales (Month 1)

### Job Description

**Title:** VP Sales
**Reports to:** CEO
**Location:** Remote (US) with quarterly in-person offsites

**Mission:** Build ARG-Builder's sales engine from zero to $5M+ ARR within 18 months. Own the entire revenue function including direct sales, partnerships, and customer expansion.

**Responsibilities:**

The VP Sales will design and execute the complete go-to-market motion for ARG-Builder, starting with founder-led sales support and scaling to a team of 5+ by end of Year 1. This person will own pipeline generation, deal execution, pricing strategy validation, and customer expansion. They will build the sales playbook, define the ideal customer profile, establish sales methodology, and hire the first account executives.

**Requirements:**

| Criterion | Must Have | Nice to Have |
|-----------|-----------|--------------|
| Experience | 8+ years B2B SaaS sales, 3+ in leadership | Mid-market enterprise experience |
| Track Record | Built sales team from 0 to $5M+ ARR | Experience at AI/ML company |
| Deal Size | Closed $25K–$100K+ ACV deals | Knowledge management domain |
| Stage | Operated at Series A or earlier | Founder experience |
| Skills | Consultative selling, enterprise sales cycles | Channel/partner development |

### Interview Scorecard

| Dimension | Weight | Assessment Method |
|-----------|--------|-------------------|
| Sales Acumen | 30% | Case study: sell ARG-Builder to a mock prospect |
| Leadership & Hiring | 25% | Behavioral: describe building a team from scratch |
| Strategic Thinking | 20% | Discussion: design GTM strategy for ARG-Builder |
| Cultural Fit | 15% | Values alignment, intellectual honesty test |
| Domain Knowledge | 10% | Understanding of mid-market operations challenges |

### Interview Process (4 stages, 10 days)

| Stage | Interviewer | Duration | Focus |
|-------|-------------|----------|-------|
| Phone Screen | CEO | 30 min | Background, motivation, salary expectations |
| Deep Dive | CEO + CTO | 60 min | Sales methodology, team building, strategy |
| Case Study | CEO | 45 min | Live role-play: sell ARG-Builder |
| Reference Checks | CEO | 3 calls | Verify track record, leadership style |

## Compensation Philosophy

ARG-Builder's compensation philosophy targets the 65th–75th percentile of market rates for base salary, supplemented by meaningful equity grants that reward early employees for taking startup risk. The total compensation package (salary + equity + benefits) should be competitive with Series A companies to attract top talent.

| Component | Approach |
|-----------|----------|
| Base Salary | 65th–75th percentile of market (Levels.fyi, Glassdoor) |
| Equity | 4-year vesting, 1-year cliff, monthly thereafter |
| Benefits | Health/dental/vision (100% employee, 50% dependents) |
| PTO | Unlimited with 3-week minimum encouraged |
| Equipment | $3,000 home office stipend |
| Learning | $2,000/year professional development budget |
| Offsites | Quarterly team gatherings (company-paid) |

### Equity Pool Allocation

| Category | Pool Allocation | Typical Grant |
|----------|----------------|---------------|
| VP/C-Level hires | 6–8% total | 1.5–2.5% each |
| Senior Engineers | 4–6% total | 0.5–1.0% each |
| Mid-Level Hires | 3–5% total | 0.2–0.5% each |
| Junior/Support | 1–2% total | 0.05–0.15% each |
| Future Hires (reserve) | 5–8% | — |

**Total Option Pool:** 15–20% (standard for seed-stage companies)

## Onboarding Framework

Every new hire goes through a structured 30-60-90 day onboarding program designed to achieve full productivity by Day 90.

| Phase | Duration | Focus | Success Metric |
|-------|----------|-------|----------------|
| Week 1 | Days 1–5 | Company context, product deep-dive, tool setup | Can demo the product |
| Week 2–4 | Days 6–30 | Role-specific training, first deliverable | Ships first contribution |
| Month 2 | Days 31–60 | Independent execution, relationship building | Owns a workstream |
| Month 3 | Days 61–90 | Full autonomy, strategic contribution | Delivering at full capacity |

## Diversity & Inclusion Commitments

ARG-Builder is committed to building a diverse team from Day 1. Specific commitments include ensuring every final-round interview slate includes at least one candidate from an underrepresented background, using structured interviews with scorecards to reduce bias, publishing salary ranges in all job postings, and conducting annual pay equity audits starting in Year 2.
