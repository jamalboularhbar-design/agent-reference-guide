# ARG Builder — LinkedIn Connection Request Templates

These templates are designed for sending connection requests to target decision-makers. LinkedIn limits connection request notes to 300 characters, so each template is optimized for that constraint. Personalize the bracketed sections before sending.

---

## Template 1: VP/Director of Operations

**For:** Operations leaders at mid-market companies (200–2,000 employees)

> Hi [First Name] — I noticed [Company] is scaling fast. I'm building an AI platform that helps ops teams keep SOPs and playbooks current as teams grow. Would love to connect and share what we're seeing in the space.

**Character count:** ~240

---

## Template 2: Director of People / HR

**For:** HR and People leaders responsible for onboarding and training documentation

> Hi [First Name] — Saw [Company] is hiring aggressively. We help People teams ensure new hires always find current, accurate operational docs on day one (not outdated wikis). Happy to share our approach if useful.

**Character count:** ~230

---

## Template 3: CTO / VP Engineering

**For:** Technical leaders who own internal tooling and documentation infrastructure

> Hi [First Name] — I'm building ARG Builder, an AI-native ops knowledge platform (think Datadog for operational docs). We auto-detect stale content, duplicates, and knowledge gaps. Would love to connect.

**Character count:** ~225

## Template 5: Mutual Connection Warm Intro

**For:** When you share a mutual connection with the prospect

> Hi [First Name] — [Mutual Name] suggested we connect. I'm working on AI-powered operational knowledge management for mid-market teams. Think it could be relevant to what [Company] is building. Looking forward to connecting.

**Character count:** ~250

## Template 10: Follow-Up After No Response (InMail)

**For:** When a connection request goes unanswered for 2+ weeks, use as an InMail

> Hi [First Name] — Quick follow-up. We help mid-market ops teams like [Company] cut 60% of time spent searching for internal docs using AI governance. Our founding customer program offers 30% off locked for 2 years. Worth a 15-min chat?

**Character count:** ~270

---

## Usage Tips

Send connection requests in batches of 15–20 per day to avoid LinkedIn restrictions. Personalize at minimum the company name and one specific detail (recent funding, hiring surge, industry event). After acceptance, wait 24 hours before sending a follow-up message. Track acceptance rates by template — aim for 30%+ acceptance rate. If a template drops below 20%, retire it and test a new variant.
