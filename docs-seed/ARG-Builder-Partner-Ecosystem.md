# ARG-Builder: Partner Ecosystem Strategy

## Framework for Building, Managing, and Scaling Strategic Partnerships

---

## 1. Executive Summary

A well-designed partner ecosystem multiplies ARG-Builder's reach, credibility, and revenue without proportional headcount growth. Partners extend distribution (channel), enhance the product (technology), deliver services (implementation), and validate market position (strategic). This document defines the complete partner ecosystem strategy — from partner identification and recruitment to enablement, co-selling, and performance management.

---

## 2. Partnership Categories

### 2.1 Partner Types

| Type | Description | Value to ARG-Builder | Value to Partner |
|------|-------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Channel/Reseller | Sell ARG-Builder to their customer base | Distribution, market access | Revenue (margin), customer value-add |
| Technology/Integration | Build integrations with ARG-Builder | Product stickiness, ecosystem | User base, joint customers |
| Implementation/Services | Deliver ARG-Builder implementations | Scalable delivery, customer success | Service revenue, differentiation |
| Strategic/Alliance | Large companies with shared customers | Brand credibility, market access | Innovation, customer retention |
| Referral | Recommend ARG-Builder, earn commission | Low-cost lead generation | Passive income, customer goodwill |
| Marketplace | List on partner's marketplace | Discovery, trust, procurement ease | Ecosystem completeness |

### 2.2 Partner Tier Structure

| Tier | Requirements | Benefits | Revenue Share | Support Level |
|------|-------------|----------|--------------|--------------|
| Platinum | > $500K influenced ARR, 5+ certifications, dedicated team | Co-marketing budget, executive access, roadmap input, priority support | 25–30% | Dedicated partner manager |
| Gold | > $200K influenced ARR, 3+ certifications | Co-marketing, joint webinars, deal registration priority | 20–25% | Named partner manager |

## 7. Co-Selling & Deal Management

### 7.1 Deal Registration Process

| Step | Action | Timeline | System |
|------|--------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Partner registers opportunity in portal | — | Partner portal |
| 2 | ARG-Builder validates (no conflict) | < 48 hours | CRM |
| 3 | Deal approved or rejected with reason | < 48 hours | Partner portal |
| 4 | 90-day protection period begins | — | Automated |
| 5 | Co-selling support activated | Ongoing | CRM |
| 6 | Deal closes (or protection expires) | — | CRM |
| 7 | Commission/margin processed | Net 30 after payment | Finance |

### 7.2 Co-Selling Model

| Scenario | ARG-Builder Role | Partner Role | Revenue Split |
|----------|-----------------|-------------|--------------|
| Partner-sourced | Support, demo assist | Lead, close | Partner margin (20–30%) |
| ARG-Builder-sourced | Lead, qualify | Implement, expand | Referral fee (10–15%) |
| Joint pursuit | Co-sell, joint demo | Co-sell, services | Negotiated per deal |
| Marketplace | Platform, billing | Listing, support | Marketplace fee (15–20%) |

### 7.3 Lead Distribution

| Criteria | Lead Routing |
|----------|-------------|

| Vertical expertise | Route to partner with industry specialization |
| Deal size | Route to partner with appropriate capacity |
| Certification level | Route to partner with required certifications |
