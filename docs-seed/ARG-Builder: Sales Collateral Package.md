## Document 5: Demo Script

### Pre-Demo Preparation (5 minutes before)

Review prospect's company, industry, team size, and known pain points. Customize demo flow to emphasize features most relevant to their situation. Prepare industry-specific examples and case study references.

### Opening (3 minutes)

"Thank you for taking the time today. Before I show you ARG-Builder, I'd like to confirm my understanding of your situation. Based on our previous conversation, you mentioned [specific pain points]. Is that still accurate? Are there any additional challenges you'd like me to address today?"

### Problem Validation (5 minutes)

"Let me share what we consistently hear from companies like yours. [Share relevant statistics about operational inconsistency, onboarding time, knowledge loss]. Does this resonate with your experience? What's the biggest operational challenge you're facing right now?"

### Solution Overview (3 minutes)

"ARG-Builder addresses these challenges through autonomous AI generation. Instead of spending months manually documenting processes, our AI agent interviews your stakeholders, maps your operations, and produces a complete interactive system in 12–19 hours. Let me show you what the end result looks like."

### Live Demo (15 minutes)

**Feature 1: Persona Navigation (3 minutes)**
"Notice how the system is organized around personas — the roles and responsibilities within your organization. Each persona has defined characteristics, communication styles, and decision frameworks. This means your team doesn't just know what to do — they know how to think and communicate in each role."

**Feature 2: Process Timelines (4 minutes)**
"Here you can see the interactive 7-stage process timeline. Each stage is clickable, showing detailed procedures, success metrics, and escalation paths. Your team can see exactly where they are in any process and what comes next."

**Feature 3: Advanced Search (3 minutes)**
"The search function indexes all content across all personas and processes. Watch how quickly results appear as I type. Your team can find any procedure, guideline, or process step in under 2 seconds."

**Feature 4: Command Palette (2 minutes)**
"For power users, the command palette (Cmd+K) provides instant access to any section. This is the same pattern used by tools like Slack, VS Code, and Linear — familiar to technical teams and incredibly efficient."

**Feature 5: PDF Export (3 minutes)**
"Everything can be exported to PDF for offline reference, compliance documentation, or sharing with external stakeholders. The exported documents maintain professional formatting and branding."

### ROI Discussion (5 minutes)

"Based on what you've shared about your organization — [team size, turnover rate, onboarding time] — let me walk through the projected ROI. [Use ROI calculator with prospect's specific numbers]. As you can see, the typical payback period is 45–60 days."

### Case Study (3 minutes)

"Let me share a quick case study from [relevant industry]. [Company name] had similar challenges — [specific pain points]. After implementing ARG-Builder, they achieved [specific metrics]. The operations director told us [relevant quote]."

### Close (5 minutes)

"Based on what we've discussed today, it seems like ARG-Builder could address [specific pain points] and deliver [specific value]. What questions do you have? What would the next steps look like on your end to evaluate this further?"

Send personalized follow-up email within 2 hours including demo recording, relevant case study, ROI calculation with prospect's numbers, proposed next steps, and timeline for decision.
