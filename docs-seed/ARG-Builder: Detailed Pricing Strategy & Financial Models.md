# ARG-Builder: Detailed Pricing Strategy & Financial Models

## Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Model Overview

ARG-Builder employs a value-based, tiered SaaS pricing model with three subscription tiers plus professional services and implementation fees. The pricing model is designed to capture value proportional to customer size and usage while remaining accessible to mid-market companies.

### Subscription Tiers

**Starter Tier: $2,000/month**

Target: Small mid-market companies (50-200 employees)

Includes:
- Up to 3 operational guides
- Up to 50 team members
- Basic search and navigation
- PDF export
- Email support
- Monthly updates and maintenance
- Community access

Ideal for: Small mid-market companies with straightforward operations, departments within larger organizations, or organizations piloting the platform.

**Professional Tier: $5,000/month**

Target: Mid-market companies (200-1,000 employees)

Includes:
- Unlimited operational guides
- Up to 500 team members
- Advanced search with filtering
- Command palette
- Interactive process timelines
- PDF and PowerPoint export
- Slack and Notion integrations
- Priority email and Slack support
- Quarterly business reviews
- Custom branding
- Community access

Ideal for: Mid-market companies with complex operations, multiple departments, and sophisticated requirements. Most customers will use this tier.

**Enterprise Tier: $10,000/month**

Target: Large enterprises (1,000+ employees) and multi-division organizations

Includes:
- Unlimited operational guides
- Unlimited team members
- All Professional tier features
- Advanced integrations (CRM, project management, analytics)
- Real-time collaboration
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- Dedicated account manager
- 24/7 phone and email support
- Custom development and integrations
- SLA guarantees (99.9% uptime)
- Advanced security and compliance
- Custom training and certification

Ideal for: Large enterprises with complex, multi-division operations, sophisticated integration requirements, and dedicated budget.

### Implementation Services Pricing

**Standard Implementation: $25,000**

Includes:
- Business discovery and requirements gathering (8 hours)
- Guide generation and customization (12 hours)
- Training and onboarding (4 hours)
- Post-launch support (2 weeks)

Timeline: 2-3 weeks

Ideal for: Straightforward organizations with clear processes and limited customization requirements.

**Custom Implementation: $50,000-$100,000**

Includes:
- Comprehensive business discovery (16-24 hours)
- Multiple guide generation and customization (24-40 hours)
- Advanced integrations and customizations (16-24 hours)
- Comprehensive training and change management (8-16 hours)
- Extended post-launch support (4-8 weeks)

Timeline: 4-8 weeks

Ideal for: Complex organizations with sophisticated requirements, multiple divisions, or significant customization needs.

### Professional Services Pricing

**Consulting Services: $150-$300/hour**

Available services:
- Operational process optimization
- Guide customization and refinement
- Organizational change management
- Training and certification
- Integration development
- Custom feature development

### Annual Commitment Discount

Customers committing to annual subscriptions receive a 15% discount, improving cash flow and customer retention. Annual subscription pricing:

- Starter: $20,400/year (vs. $24,000 monthly)
- Professional: $51,000/year (vs. $60,000 monthly)
- Enterprise: $102,000/year (vs. $120,000 monthly)

### Volume Discounts

Organizations with multiple divisions or subsidiaries receive volume discounts:

- 2-5 guides: 10% discount
- 6-10 guides: 15% discount
- 10+ guides: 20% discount

### Unit Economics Analysis

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**

Year 1 CAC = Marketing Spend / New Customers = $192,000 / 54 = $3,556 per customer

As the company scales and brand awareness grows, CAC decreases:
- Year 2 CAC: $2,500 (improved marketing efficiency)
- Year 3 CAC: $2,000 (strong brand and word-of-mouth)
- Year 4-5 CAC: $1,500 (mature market position)

**Average Contract Value (ACV):**

Year 1 ACV = Total Revenue / New Customers = $2,174,000 / 54 = $40,259

ACV includes both subscription and implementation services. Pure subscription ACV:
- Year 1: $20,815 (average subscription price $4,000/month × 5.2 months)
- Year 2: $21,333 (customers upgrade to higher tiers)
- Year 3: $24,000 (continued tier upgrades)
- Year 4: $24,000 (mature tier mix)
- Year 5: $24,000 (stable tier mix)

**Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):**

LTV = (ACV × Gross Margin) / Monthly Churn Rate

Year 1 LTV (assuming 2% monthly churn):
- LTV = ($20,815 × 0.56) / 0.02 = $581,620

This assumes customers stay with ARG-Builder for approximately 50 months (4+ years) on average.

**LTV/CAC Ratio:**

Year 1 LTV/CAC = $581,620 / $3,556 = 164x

This exceptional ratio indicates highly efficient customer acquisition. Industry benchmark for healthy SaaS is 3x+, so ARG-Builder's unit economics are excellent.

**CAC Payback Period:**

Year 1 CAC Payback = CAC / (Monthly Subscription Revenue / Customer)

CAC Payback = $3,556 / ($20,815 / 12) = $3,556 / $1,735 = 2.1 months

This means the company recovers customer acquisition costs in approximately 2 months, enabling rapid reinvestment in growth.
