## Interview Questions (for gathering case study content)

Use these questions during the 30-minute champion interview:

1. What was your operational knowledge situation like before ARG Builder? What specific problems were you experiencing?
2. How did you discover ARG Builder? What alternatives did you evaluate?
3. What was the deciding factor that made you choose ARG Builder?
4. Walk me through the implementation process. What surprised you (positively or negatively)?
5. What results have you seen so far? Can you quantify the improvement?
6. Which specific feature has been most valuable to your team?
7. How has your team's relationship with documentation changed?
8. What would you say to someone considering ARG Builder?
9. What are your plans for expanding usage?
10. Is there anything you wish we did differently?
