# ARG-Builder: Sales Demo Playbook

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's structured demo methodology — the repeatable framework for delivering compelling product demonstrations that convert prospects into customers. A great demo is not a feature tour; it is a consultative conversation that connects the prospect's specific pain to a tangible solution, creating an emotional "aha moment" that makes the buying decision feel inevitable.

---

## Demo Philosophy

### Core Principles

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Discovery before demo** | Never demo without understanding the prospect's pain first |
| **Tell, show, tell** | Frame the problem → demonstrate the solution → reinforce the value |
| **Personalize everything** | Use their industry, terminology, and scenarios |
| **Less is more** | Show 3 features deeply, not 10 features superficially |
| **Create the "aha moment"** | Every demo must produce one moment of genuine surprise/delight |
| **End with next steps** | Never end without a clear, committed next action |

## Pre-Demo Preparation

### Discovery Questions (Before Demo)

| Category | Questions | Purpose |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Pain | "What's the biggest operational challenge you're facing right now?" | Identify primary pain |
| Impact | "How is this affecting your team's productivity/revenue/quality?" | Quantify the cost |
| Current state | "How are you handling this today? What tools are you using?" | Understand baseline |
| Stakeholders | "Who else is involved in this decision? What do they care about?" | Map buying committee |
| Timeline | "What's driving the urgency? Is there a deadline or event?" | Assess timeline |
| Success | "If you solved this perfectly, what would that look like in 6 months?" | Define desired outcome |
| Budget | "Have you allocated budget for solving this? What range?" | Qualify financially |

## Demo Structure (30 Minutes)

### Timing Framework

| Section | Duration | Purpose | Key Actions |
|---------|----------|---------|-------------|
| Opening & agenda | 3 min | Set expectations, confirm attendees | Confirm goals, introduce team |
| Pain recap | 5 min | Demonstrate understanding, build trust | Summarize discovery, confirm priorities |
| Solution demo | 15 min | Show product solving their specific problem | 3 features, personalized scenarios |
| Social proof | 3 min | Build credibility with similar customer | Case study, metrics, quote |
| Next steps | 4 min | Advance the deal | Propose specific next action |

### Demo Flow (15-Minute Core)

| Segment | Duration | Content | Transition |
|---------|----------|---------|-----------|
| **Segment 1: The Problem** | 2 min | "Let me show you what [pain] looks like today..." | Show the chaos/inefficiency |
| **Segment 2: The Solution** | 5 min | "Now let me show you how ARG-Builder solves this..." | Core feature demonstration |
| **Segment 3: The Power** | 5 min | "But here's where it gets really powerful..." | Advanced feature (search, timeline, command palette) |
| **Segment 4: The Result** | 3 min | "And here's the outcome your team experiences..." | Show completed guide, team using it |

## Objection Handling During Demo

### Common Demo Objections

| Objection | Response Framework | Supporting Evidence |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------------|
| "We already have Confluence/Notion" | "Those are great for general docs. ARG-Builder is purpose-built for operational reference — structured personas, process timelines, and instant search that general tools can't match." | Feature comparison, switching case study |
| "This looks complex" | "Let me show you — [create a persona in 60 seconds]. Most teams are fully set up in under a day." | Live demo of simplicity, time-to-value data |
| "Our team won't adopt it" | "That's the #1 concern we hear. Here's why 92% of teams achieve full adoption within 2 weeks: [show search + command palette]" | Adoption metrics, ease-of-use demo |
| "We can build this ourselves" | "You absolutely could. The question is: should your team spend 6 months building a tool, or 1 day deploying one and getting back to their core work?" | Build vs. buy analysis, opportunity cost |
| "What about security?" | "Great question. [Show security page]. SOC 2 Type II, encryption at rest and in transit, SSO, role-based access." | Compliance documentation |
