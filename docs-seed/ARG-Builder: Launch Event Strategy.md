# ARG-Builder: Launch Event Strategy

## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive launch strategy for ARG-Builder's public market entry, designed to generate maximum awareness, pipeline, and credibility within the first 30 days of public launch. ## Channel 1: Product Hunt Launch

### Preparation (Weeks -3 to -1)

Product Hunt remains the most effective single-day launch channel for B2B SaaS products targeting early adopters and technology-forward buyers. A top-5 finish on launch day generates 2,000–5,000 website visits and 50–200 signups.

**Pre-Launch Checklist:**

| Task | Timeline | Owner |
|------|----------|-------|
| Create Product Hunt maker profile | Week -3 | CEO |
| Build "Coming Soon" page with email capture | Week -3 | Marketing |
| Recruit 5 hunters with 1,000+ followers | Week -3 | CEO |
| Prepare all launch assets (images, video, copy) | Week -2 | Marketing |
| Engage Product Hunt community (upvote, comment) | Weeks -3 to -1 | All team |
| Schedule launch for Tuesday (highest traffic day) | Week -1 | CEO |
| Prepare launch day support team (5+ people) | Week -1 | All team |
| Draft responses to common questions | Week -1 | CEO + Product |

**Launch Day Assets:**

| Asset | Specifications |
|-------|---------------|
| Tagline | "AI generates your operational playbook in 12 hours — not 12 months" |
| Description (260 chars) | "ARG-Builder autonomously interviews your team, maps processes, and delivers an interactive operational system with 92% adoption. Replace $180K consulting projects with a 12-hour AI-powered solution." |
| Gallery Images | 5 images: Dashboard, AI Interview, Guide Builder, Deployed Guide, Analytics |
| Demo Video | 90-second product walkthrough (no audio, captions only) |
| Maker Comment | 500-word founder story explaining why you built this |

**Launch Day Execution (Tuesday, 12:01 AM PT):**

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| 12:01 AM | Product goes live, maker comment posted |
| 6:00 AM | Email blast to waitlist: "We're live on Product Hunt!" |
| 7:00 AM | LinkedIn post from CEO with personal story |
| 8:00 AM | Tweet/X thread with product screenshots |
| 9:00 AM–6:00 PM | Respond to every comment within 15 minutes |
| 12:00 PM | Second LinkedIn post with early traction metrics |
| 3:00 PM | Email to network asking for support |
| 6:00 PM | Thank-you post on Product Hunt |
| 9:00 PM | Wrap-up: share final ranking and metrics |

**Target Outcome:** Top 5 Product of the Day, 200+ upvotes, 3,000+ website visits, 100+ signups.

## Channel 2: Press & Media Outreach

### Target Publications

| Tier | Publications | Approach |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Tier 1 (Dream) | TechCrunch, VentureBeat, Forbes | Exclusive story offer |
| Tier 2 (Likely) | SaaStr, Built In, Business Insider | Press release + pitch |
| Tier 3 (Guaranteed) | Industry blogs, podcasts, newsletters | Guest posts, interviews |

### Press Angles

**Angle 1: "The $2.8M Problem No One's Solving"**
Frame: Industry trend piece about mid-market operational chaos, with ARG-Builder as the solution. Best for TechCrunch, VentureBeat.

**Angle 2: "AI Replaces $180K Consulting Projects in 12 Hours"**
Frame: Disruption story — AI vs. traditional consulting. Provocative, attention-grabbing. Best for Forbes, Business Insider.

**Angle 3: "92% Adoption Where Consulting Fails at 15%"**
Frame: Data-driven product story focused on the adoption breakthrough. Best for SaaStr, product-focused publications.

### Press Release Structure

**Headline:** ARG-Builder Launches AI Platform That Generates Complete Operational Systems in 12 Hours, Achieving 92% Team Adoption

**Subhead:** New AI-powered SaaS platform replaces $180K consulting projects for mid-market companies, delivering interactive operational guides with unprecedented adoption rates

**Body Structure:**
1. Lead paragraph (who, what, when, why)
2. Problem statement with market data ($2.8M annual loss)
3. Solution description with key differentiators
4. Case study results (35% efficiency, 92% adoption)
5. Founder quote on vision and mission
6. Customer quote on impact
7. Pricing and availability
8. Company background and funding status

### Journalist Outreach Template

**Subject:** Exclusive: AI platform generates operational systems 200x faster than consulting firms

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out because your recent piece on [relevant article] resonated with something we're launching next week.

ARG-Builder is an AI platform that autonomously generates complete interactive operational systems for mid-market companies in 12–19 hours. Our early customers were spending $180K+ on consulting firms and getting 15% adoption. We're delivering 92% adoption at a fraction of the cost.

I'd love to offer you an exclusive look before our public launch on [date]. I can provide:
- Early access to the platform
- Customer interviews (3 case studies with metrics)
- Founder interview and background

Would you be interested in covering this? Happy to work around your schedule.

Best,
[Founder Name]

### Engagement Strategy

The approach is value-first, promotion-second. Spend 4 weeks providing genuine value in communities before any product mention. Share insights, answer questions, and build relationships. When launch day arrives, the community already knows and trusts you.

| Week | Activity | Goal |
|------|----------|------|
| Week -4 | Join communities, introduce yourself | Establish presence |
| Week -3 | Answer 5+ questions per community | Build credibility |
| Week -2 | Share original insights/data | Demonstrate expertise |
| Week -1 | Mention upcoming launch casually | Build anticipation |
| Launch Week | Share launch with context and value | Drive traffic |
| Post-Launch | Continue value contribution | Sustain relationships |
