## Governance & Ethics

### Ethical AI Principles

| Principle | Commitment | Implementation |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| Transparency | Customers know when AI is generating content | Clear AI labeling, disclosure |
| Fairness | AI does not discriminate or perpetuate bias | Bias testing, diverse training data |
| Privacy | Customer data never used to train models without consent | Opt-in only, data isolation |
| Accountability | Humans responsible for AI outputs | Human review workflows, audit trails |
| Safety | AI cannot generate harmful content | Content filters, safety guardrails |
| Explainability | AI decisions can be understood | Explanation features, documentation |

### Corporate Ethics

| Area | Policy | Enforcement |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Anti-corruption | Zero tolerance for bribery, kickbacks | Annual training, whistleblower hotline |
| Conflicts of interest | Disclosure required, recusal when needed | Annual disclosure form |
| Data ethics | Customer data is sacred, never sold | Technical controls, audit |
| Fair competition | Compete on merit, no disparagement | Marketing review process |
| Responsible marketing | No misleading claims, honest metrics | Legal review of all claims |
| Supplier ethics | Require ethical standards from vendors | Vendor code of conduct |
