# ARG-Builder: Board of Directors Governance

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's Board of Directors governance framework — the composition strategy, meeting cadence, committee structure, information rights, decision-making protocols, and fiduciary responsibilities that ensure effective governance as the company scales from seed through Series B and beyond. Effective board governance balances accountability with operational speed, providing strategic oversight without micromanagement.

## Meeting Cadence & Structure

### Board Meeting Schedule

| Meeting Type | Frequency | Duration | Format | Attendees |
|-------------|-----------|----------|--------|-----------|
| Full board meeting | Quarterly | 3 hours | In-person preferred | All directors + CEO + CFO |
| Committee meetings | As needed | 1 hour | Video | Committee members |
| Board dinner (informal) | Semi-annually | 2 hours | In-person | All directors + leadership |
| Emergency session | As needed | 1 hour | Video | All directors |
| Annual strategy session | Annually | Full day | In-person | Directors + leadership team |

### Standard Board Meeting Agenda

| Section | Duration | Content | Pre-Read Required |
|---------|----------|---------|-------------------|
| Opening & minutes approval | 10 min | Approve prior minutes, declare conflicts | Prior minutes |
| CEO update | 30 min | Strategy, key decisions, challenges | CEO letter (2–3 pages) |
| Financial review | 30 min | P&L, cash, ARR, burn, runway | Financial package |
| Product & engineering | 20 min | Roadmap progress, key metrics | Product update |
| Sales & marketing | 20 min | Pipeline, wins/losses, marketing metrics | GTM dashboard |
| Strategic discussion | 45 min | 1–2 deep-dive topics (pre-selected) | Topic briefing docs |
| Closed session | 15 min | Board-only (no management) | — |
| Action items & close | 10 min | Summarize decisions, assign follow-ups | — |

### Board Package (Sent 5 Business Days Prior)

| Document | Pages | Content |
|----------|-------|---------|

## Decision Rights Matrix

### Board Approval Required

| Decision | Threshold | Approval Type |
|----------|-----------|---------------|
| Annual budget | All budgets | Board approval |
| Fundraising (equity) | Any amount | Board + shareholder approval |
| Debt financing | > $500K | Board approval |
| M&A (acquisition) | Any | Board + shareholder approval |
| Executive hiring (C-suite) | All | Board approval |
| Executive termination (C-suite) | All | Board approval |
| Equity grants (> 1% pool) | > 1% of authorized shares | Board approval |
| Material contracts | > $500K annual value | Board approval |
| Litigation (initiating) | > $100K potential exposure | Board approval |
| Change of business direction | Material pivot | Board approval |
| Related-party transactions | Any amount | Board approval (conflicted director recused) |

### CEO Authority (No Board Approval Needed)

| Decision | Limit |
|----------|-------|
| Operating expenditures | Within approved budget |
| Hiring (non-executive) | Within headcount plan |
| Customer contracts | Standard terms, any size |
| Vendor contracts | < $500K annual |
| Equity grants (routine) | Within board-approved pool and guidelines |
| Product decisions | All (within strategy) |
| Pricing changes | Within approved framework |
| Partnership agreements | Non-exclusive, < $250K commitment |
