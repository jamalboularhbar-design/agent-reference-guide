# ARG-Builder: Account-Based Marketing (ABM) Strategy

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's Account-Based Marketing strategy — the coordinated, multi-channel approach to engaging high-value target accounts with personalized campaigns that drive enterprise pipeline. ABM flips the traditional funnel by starting with ideal accounts and orchestrating tailored experiences across marketing, sales, and customer success to win and expand strategic relationships.

---

## ABM Program Structure

### Tiered ABM Approach

| Tier | Name | Accounts | Personalization | Investment/Account | Team |
|------|------|----------|----------------|-------------------|------|
| **1:1** | Strategic ABM | 10–25 | Fully custom campaigns per account | $5K–$15K/account/quarter | Dedicated AE + Marketing |
| **1:Few** | Cluster ABM | 50–100 | Industry/segment-specific campaigns | $1K–$3K/account/quarter | AE + Shared marketing |
| **1:Many** | Programmatic ABM | 200–500 | Automated, personalized at scale | $200–$500/account/quarter | Automated + SDR |

## Account Selection

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Scoring

| Criterion | Weight | Score 5 | Score 3 | Score 1 |
|-----------|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Company size (employees) | 20% | 500–5,000 | 200–499 or 5,001–10,000 | < 200 or > 10,000 |
| Revenue | 15% | $50M–$500M | $20M–$50M or $500M–$1B | < $20M or > $1B |

| Technology stack | 15% | Modern (cloud-native, API-first) | Mixed (some legacy) | Legacy-heavy |

| Operational complexity | 10% | Multi-department, distributed teams | Growing complexity | Simple operations |
| Budget authority | 10% | VP+ with discretionary budget | Director-level | Manager or below |

## 1:Many Programmatic ABM (200–500 Accounts)

### Automation Framework

| Stage | Trigger | Automated Action | Personalization |
|-------|---------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Awareness | Account matches ICP + shows intent | Display ads + LinkedIn ads served | Company name, industry |
| Engagement | Account visits website (2+ people) | Personalized email sequence triggered | Industry content, company name |
| Consideration | Account engages with 3+ content pieces | SDR alert + personalized outreach | Specific content consumed, pain hypothesis |
| Evaluation | Account requests demo or pricing | AE assignment + custom demo prep | Full account intelligence briefing |
