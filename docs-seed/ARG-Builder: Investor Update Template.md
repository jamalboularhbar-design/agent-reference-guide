# ARG-Builder: Investor Update Template

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's investor communication framework — the structured templates and cadence for keeping investors informed, engaged, and supportive. Consistent, transparent investor updates build trust, unlock warm introductions, and position the company favorably for future fundraising rounds.

---

## Update Cadence

### Communication Schedule

| Type | Frequency | Audience | Length | Purpose |
|------|-----------|----------|--------|---------|
| Monthly update | Monthly (1st week) | All investors + advisors | 1 page | Progress, metrics, asks |
| Quarterly deep-dive | Quarterly | Lead investors + board | 3–5 pages | Strategy, financials, decisions |
| Annual review | Annually (January) | All investors | 5–8 pages | Year in review, next year plan |
| Ad-hoc (milestone) | As needed | All investors | 0.5 page | Major wins, press, fundraising |

## Best Practices

### Do's and Don'ts

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Send on a consistent date (1st of month) | Skip months or send inconsistently |
| Lead with metrics and be transparent | Hide bad news or spin negatively |
| Include specific, actionable asks | Make vague requests ("help us grow") |
| Keep it to 1 page (monthly) | Write 5-page monthly novels |
| Celebrate wins AND acknowledge challenges | Only share good news |
| Respond to investor replies within 24 hours | Ignore investor responses |
| Track which investors help with asks | Forget to thank investors who help |
| Include forward-looking priorities | Only look backward |
