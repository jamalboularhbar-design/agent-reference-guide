## Document 5: Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

### ARG-Builder Data Processing Agreement

**1. Definitions**

"Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person. "Processing" means any operation performed on Personal Data. "Controller" means the Customer who determines the purposes and means of Processing. "Processor" means ARG-Builder who processes Personal Data on behalf of the Controller.

**2. Scope of Processing**

ARG-Builder processes Personal Data solely for the purpose of providing the Service as described in the Customer Agreement. Categories of data subjects include Customer employees, Customer clients (if included in guide content), and Customer vendors/partners (if included in guide content). Categories of Personal Data include names, roles, contact information, and business process information.

**3. Security Measures**

ARG-Builder implements appropriate technical and organizational measures including encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+), access controls and authentication, regular security testing, employee security training, incident response procedures, and business continuity planning.

**4. Sub-Processors**

ARG-Builder uses the following sub-processors:

| Sub-Processor | Purpose | Location |
|---------------|---------|----------|
| Amazon Web Services | Cloud infrastructure | US |
| OpenAI | AI model processing | US |
| Anthropic | AI model processing | US |
| Stripe | Payment processing | US |
| SendGrid | Email delivery | US |

Customer is notified 30 days in advance of new sub-processor additions and may object within 15 days.

**5. Data Transfers**

Personal Data is processed in US-based data centers. For transfers from the EU/EEA, ARG-Builder relies on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as approved by the European Commission.

**6. Data Subject Rights**

ARG-Builder assists Customer in responding to data subject requests including access, rectification, erasure, portability, and objection. Requests are fulfilled within 30 days.

**7. Breach Notification**

ARG-Builder notifies Customer of Personal Data breaches without undue delay and no later than 72 hours after becoming aware. Notification includes nature of breach, categories of data affected, likely consequences, and measures taken to address the breach.

**8. Audit Rights**

Customer may audit ARG-Builder's compliance with this DPA once per year with 30 days advance notice. ARG-Builder provides SOC 2 Type II reports as evidence of compliance.

### Compliance Implementation Steps

**Phase 1 (Months 1–3): Foundation**

Implement security policies and procedures. Deploy encryption, access controls, and audit logging. Conduct initial risk assessment. Develop incident response plan. Implement employee security training. Self-certify GDPR and CCPA compliance.

**Phase 2 (Months 4–6): SOC 2 Preparation**

Engage SOC 2 auditor. Implement required controls. Document policies and procedures. Conduct internal audit. Address gaps and remediate findings. Complete SOC 2 Type I audit.

**Phase 3 (Months 7–12): Advanced Compliance**

Implement HIPAA controls for healthcare customers. Maintain SOC 2 controls for 6+ months. Complete SOC 2 Type II audit. Begin ISO 27001 preparation. Conduct annual risk assessment.

**Phase 4 (Months 13–18): Enterprise Compliance**

Complete ISO 27001 certification. Implement advanced security monitoring. Conduct penetration testing. Develop compliance automation. Establish ongoing compliance program.
