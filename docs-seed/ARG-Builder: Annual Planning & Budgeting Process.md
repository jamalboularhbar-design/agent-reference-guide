# ARG-Builder: Annual Planning & Budgeting Process

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's annual planning and budgeting process — the structured methodology for setting company strategy, defining objectives, allocating resources, and establishing financial targets for the fiscal year. Effective annual planning translates long-term vision into executable quarterly plans, aligns the entire organization around shared priorities, and creates accountability through measurable outcomes.

---

## Planning Calendar

### Annual Planning Timeline (October–January)

| Phase | Timing | Duration | Activities | Output |
|-------|--------|----------|-----------|--------|
| **Reflection** | Oct 1–15 | 2 weeks | Year-in-review, lessons learned, market analysis | Retrospective document |

## Quarterly Review Process

### Quarterly Business Review (QBR)

| Section | Duration | Content | Presenter |
|---------|----------|---------|-----------|
| Financial review | 20 min | Revenue, expenses, cash vs. plan | VP Finance |
| OKR progress | 30 min | Company OKR scoring (0–1.0), commentary | CEO |
| Department highlights | 30 min | Key wins, challenges, asks per department | Department heads |
| Strategic discussion | 20 min | Market changes, strategy adjustments | CEO |
| Next quarter planning | 20 min | Q+1 priorities, resource adjustments | CEO |

### OKR Scoring

| Score | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 0.0–0.3 | Failed to make meaningful progress | Root cause analysis, plan adjustment |
| 0.4–0.6 | Made progress but fell short | Evaluate if goal was too ambitious or execution failed |
| 0.7–1.0 | Achieved or exceeded target | Celebrate, set more ambitious next quarter |

## Budget Management (In-Year)

### Variance Reporting

| Variance Level | Action Required | Approver |
|---------------|----------------|----------|
| < 5% over budget | No action, note in monthly report | Department head |
| 5–15% over budget | Written justification, reallocation plan | VP Finance |
| 15–25% over budget | Formal budget revision request | CEO |
| > 25% over budget | Board notification, emergency review | CEO + Board |

### Reforecasting Cadence

| Frequency | Scope | Purpose | Owner |
|-----------|-------|---------|-------|
| Monthly | Revenue + cash | Track actuals vs. plan | Finance |
| Quarterly | Full P&L | Adjust annual forecast | Finance + Leadership |
| Ad hoc | Specific scenarios | Material changes (new funding, market shift) | CEO + Finance |
