# ARG-Builder: Customer Lifecycle Email Strategy

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's complete lifecycle email strategy — the automated communication system that nurtures prospects, activates users, retains customers, and drives expansion revenue across every stage of the customer journey. Each email is purpose-built to move recipients toward the next milestone in their relationship with ARG-Builder.

---

## Lifecycle Stages & Email Programs

| Stage | Goal | Duration | Emails | Primary Metric |
|-------|------|----------|--------|---------------|
| **Awareness** | Educate and capture interest | Ongoing | Newsletter, blog digests | Open rate, CTR |
| **Consideration** | Build trust and demonstrate value | 2–4 weeks | Nurture sequence (7 emails) | MQL conversion |
| **Trial** | Activate and convert | 14 days | Onboarding sequence (10 emails) | Trial-to-paid |
| **Onboarding** | Ensure successful adoption | 30 days | Success sequence (8 emails) | Activation milestones |

| **Advocacy** | Generate referrals and testimonials | Post-success | Advocacy sequence (4 emails) | Referrals, reviews |
| **Win-back** | Re-engage churned customers | Post-churn | Win-back sequence (5 emails) | Reactivation rate |

## Stage 1: Awareness Emails

### Newsletter (Bi-weekly)

| Element | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| **Subject line pattern** | "[Insight] + [Benefit]" — e.g., "Why 73% of ops teams fail at documentation (and how to fix it)" |
| **Content structure** | 1 featured article + 2 quick tips + 1 customer spotlight + 1 CTA |
| **Tone** | Educational, authoritative, not salesy |
| **CTA** | Soft — "Read more," "See how [Company] did it," "Try free" |
| **Segment** | All subscribers (non-customers) |
| **Target metrics** | 35%+ open rate, 5%+ CTR |
