# ARG-Builder: Strategic Advisory Board Design

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's Strategic Advisory Board — the composition, recruitment strategy, compensation model, meeting cadence, governance structure, and value exchange framework that creates a high-impact advisory network. A well-designed advisory board provides domain expertise, industry connections, credibility, and strategic guidance that accelerates growth without the governance overhead of a formal board of directors.

## Advisory Board Purpose

### Strategic Objectives

| Objective | How Advisors Help | Measurable Outcome |
|-----------|------------------|-------------------|

| Fundraising support | Investor introductions, reference calls | Higher valuation, faster close |
| Talent acquisition | Refer top candidates, help close hires | 2–3 referrals per advisor per year |
| Strategic guidance | Pattern matching from prior experience | Avoid costly mistakes |

## Compensation Model

### Equity Compensation

| Tier | Equity Grant | Vesting | Time Commitment | Profile |
|------|-------------|---------|-----------------|---------|
| Strategic Advisor | 0.25%–0.50% | 2 years, monthly | 4–6 hours/month | Deep engagement, regular meetings |
| Expert Advisor | 0.10%–0.25% | 2 years, monthly | 2–4 hours/month | Domain expertise, periodic input |
| Network Advisor | 0.05%–0.10% | 1 year, monthly | 1–2 hours/month | Introductions, light guidance |

### Value Exchange Framework

| What Advisors Give | What Advisors Receive |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| 4–6 hours/month of time | Equity upside (0.10%–0.50%) |
| Industry expertise and pattern matching | Early access to innovative AI product |
| Customer/investor introductions | Association with high-growth company |
| Hiring referrals | Expanded professional network |
| Brand credibility (bio on website) | Advisory title for their portfolio |
| Honest, candid feedback | Learning about AI/SaaS trends |

### Cash Compensation (Optional)

| Scenario | Cash Component | When to Use |
|----------|---------------|-------------|
| Pre-revenue startup | Equity only | Default for early stage |
| Post-revenue, pre-profit | $500–$1,000/meeting | High-value advisors who need cash |
| Profitable company | $2,000–$5,000/quarter | Retain top advisors long-term |
| Specific project work | $200–$500/hour | Defined scope beyond advisory |

## Meeting Structure

### Advisory Board Meetings (Quarterly)

| Section | Duration | Content | Preparation |
|---------|----------|---------|-------------|
| Company update | 20 min | Metrics, progress, key decisions | Pre-read deck (sent 5 days prior) |
| Strategic discussion | 40 min | 1–2 deep-dive topics | Discussion questions shared in advance |
| Advisor input | 30 min | Feedback, suggestions, connections | Advisors prepare thoughts on topics |
| Action items | 10 min | Clear next steps with owners | Documented and followed up |

### Individual Advisor Meetings (Monthly)

| Format | Duration | Content |
|--------|----------|---------|
| 1:1 with CEO or relevant executive | 30–45 min | Specific questions, introductions, feedback |
| Async update (months without meeting) | 5 min read | Written update with specific asks |

### Annual Advisory Summit (In-Person)

| Day | Content | Purpose |
|-----|---------|---------|
| Day 1 (half day) | Company deep-dive, product demo, team introductions | Context and relationship building |
| Day 1 (evening) | Dinner with leadership team | Informal relationship building |
| Day 2 (half day) | Strategic planning workshop, advisor-led sessions | Strategic input and planning |

## Governance & Operations

### Advisory Agreement Key Terms

| Term | Standard |
|------|----------|
| Duration | 2 years, renewable by mutual agreement |
| Termination | Either party, 30 days written notice |
| Confidentiality | Standard NDA covering all company information |
| Non-compete | None (advisors may advise others) |
| Non-solicitation | 12 months (employees only) |
| IP assignment | Any IP created during advisory belongs to company |
| Indemnification | Company indemnifies advisor for advisory activities |
| Liability | Advisor not liable for company decisions |

## Advisory Board vs. Board of Directors

| Dimension | Advisory Board | Board of Directors |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| Legal authority | None (advisory only) | Fiduciary duty, governance authority |
| Liability | None | Personal liability (D&O insurance) |
| Compensation | Equity (0.05%–0.50%) | Equity (0.25%–1.0%) + cash |
| Commitment | 2–6 hours/month | 10–20 hours/month |
| Meeting frequency | Quarterly | Monthly–quarterly |
| Voting rights | None | Yes (major decisions) |
| Removal | Easy (30 days notice) | Complex (shareholder vote) |
| Best for | Expertise, network, credibility | Governance, fundraising, accountability |
