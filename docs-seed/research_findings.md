# Research Findings: Luxury Travel & Creative Studio Operations

## 1. Luxury Travel (Morocco Focus)
### Target Audience: HNW Americans
- **Expectations:** High-touch service, 24/7 availability, extreme privacy, security, and "money-can't-buy" experiences.
- **Key Services:**
    - Private Jet/Helicopter charters.
    - Exclusive access (e.g., private museum viewings, closed-door garden tours).
    - Luxury Riads (e.g., Royal Mansour, L'Hôtel Marrakech).
    - Bespoke Sahara camps (e.g., Dar Ahlam).
    - Curated Atlas Mountain experiences (Berber culture, private hiking).
    - Michelin-star dining and private chefs.
- **Operational Processes:**
    - **Inquiry/Onboarding:** Detailed vetting (net worth, residences, travel budget).
    - **Itinerary Design:** Bespoke, multi-day, high-detail planning.
    - **Concierge:** 24/7 support via text/email, proactive problem solving.
    - **Security:** Coordination of private security and secure transport.

## 2. Creative Studio (ArtKech Focus)
### Core Services: Brand Identity, Editorial Design, Photography, Independent Publishing.
- **Value Proposition:** "Shelf presence," "premium pricing," "design quality," "books that look like they cost twice what they sell for."
- **Key Workflow (A to Z):**
    - **Problem Identification:** Starting with a reader problem.
    - **Concept & Strategy:** Brand identity and editorial vision.
    - **Production:** Photography production, high-end printing/binding standards.
    - **Publishing:** Specialty titles, journals, activity books.
- **Operational Processes:**
    - **Client Onboarding:** Trust-building, clear communication, strategy calls.
    - **Project Management:** End-to-end management from concept to physical object.
    - **Quality Control:** Attention to craft comparable to traditional publishing houses.

## 3. Unified Agent Requirements
- **Tone:** Professional, sophisticated, authoritative yet supportive, meticulous.
- **Capabilities:**
    - Handle HNW travel inquiries and itinerary drafting.
    - Manage creative project lifecycles (briefing to production).
    - Switch between "Luxury Travel Concierge" and "Creative Studio Director" personas.
    - Maintain a high standard of "polish" in all communications.
