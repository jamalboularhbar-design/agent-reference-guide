# ARG Builder — 60-Second Demo Video Script

**Format:** Loom screen recording with face cam in corner
**Duration:** 55–65 seconds
**Tone:** Confident, conversational, founder energy
**Background:** ARG Builder dashboard open, dark theme

---

## Script

**[0:00–0:05] Hook (face cam full screen)**

"Your team has 500 documents scattered across 6 tools. Nobody reads them. Nobody trusts them. Here's how we fix that in 60 seconds."

**[0:05–0:15] The Problem (screen: show a messy Google Drive or Notion with scattered docs)**

"This is what most mid-market ops teams deal with — SOPs in Google Docs, playbooks in Notion, onboarding guides in Confluence. When someone needs an answer, they Slack a colleague instead of searching. That costs you $4.3 million a year in lost productivity."

**[0:15–0:30] The Platform (screen: ARG Builder dashboard, click through features)**

"ARG Builder turns all of that into a single, AI-governed intelligence layer."

- *(click)* "AI generates and maintains your documents — flags stale content, detects duplicates, suggests improvements."
- *(click)* "The knowledge graph shows how everything connects — find gaps before they become incidents."
- *(click)* "Reading analytics tell you exactly who read what, when, and whether they understood it."

**[0:30–0:45] The Result (screen: show analytics dashboard with engagement metrics)**

"Within 30 days, our pilot customers see 60% less time searching for information, 40% fewer repeated questions in Slack, and full compliance visibility for audits."

**[0:45–0:55] CTA (face cam full screen)**

"We're taking 20 founding customers right now — 30% off locked for 24 months, direct access to me, and quarterly roadmap input. Link in the description to book a 30-minute personalized demo."

**[0:55–0:60] End card**

*ARG Builder logo + "argbuilder.io" + "Book Your Demo" button*

## Recording Tips

1. Record in one take — imperfection feels authentic for a founder video
2. Keep your energy up but not salesy — you're showing, not pitching
3. Use keyboard shortcuts to navigate the app quickly (Cmd+K command palette)
4. Have the knowledge graph pre-loaded with real Riad & Routes data so it looks populated
5. Ensure dark theme is on — it looks more premium on video
6. Record at 1080p minimum, 4K preferred for LinkedIn native upload
7. Add captions in post-production (85% of LinkedIn video is watched on mute)
