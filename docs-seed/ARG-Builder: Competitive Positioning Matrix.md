## Win/Loss Patterns

### When We Win

| Scenario | Why We Win | Key Differentiator |
|----------|-----------|-------------------|
| Team adoption is critical | 92% adoption rate vs. 30% industry average | Purpose-built UX, instant search |
| Speed to value matters | Live in days, not months | AI-powered generation, templates |
| Multiple departments involved | Persona-based structure scales across teams | Multi-persona architecture |
| Executive visibility needed | Process timelines, analytics | Built-in reporting |
| Existing tools failed | Proven adoption where others didn't stick | Change management built-in |

### When We Lose

| Scenario | Why We Lose | Counter-Strategy |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Price-only decision | We're premium-priced | Emphasize TCO and ROI, not unit price |
| Already deep in Notion/Confluence | Switching cost too high | Integration approach, not replacement |
| Need workflow automation | We're reference, not execution | Partner with process tools, position as complementary |
| Very small team (< 5) | Hard to justify cost | Self-serve tier, PLG motion |
| Highly regulated (need specific compliance) | May lack specific certifications | Accelerate compliance roadmap |
