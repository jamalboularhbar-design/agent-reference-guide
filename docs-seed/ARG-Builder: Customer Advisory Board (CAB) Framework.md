# ARG-Builder: Customer Advisory Board (CAB) Framework

## Executive Summary

A Customer Advisory Board is a strategic asset that accelerates product-market fit, strengthens customer relationships, reduces churn among participants to near-zero, and provides authentic social proof for sales. This framework defines the structure, recruitment, governance, meeting cadence, and value exchange for ARG-Builder's CAB — designed to launch at 15 customers and scale to 25 members by Year 2.

---

## CAB Purpose & Objectives

The CAB serves four strategic functions, each with measurable outcomes:

| Function | Objective | Success Metric |
|----------|-----------|----------------|
| Product Direction | Validate roadmap priorities with real customer input | 80%+ alignment between CAB feedback and shipped features |
| Market Intelligence | Understand evolving customer needs and competitive dynamics | 3+ actionable insights per meeting |
| Relationship Deepening | Create executive-level bonds that prevent churn | 0% churn among CAB members |
| Social Proof | Generate testimonials, case studies, and references | 2+ new references per quarter |

## CAB Structure

### Membership Composition

| Attribute | Requirement | Rationale |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Total members | 12–15 (Year 1), 20–25 (Year 2) | Small enough for meaningful discussion |
| Industry mix | 4–5 industries represented | Diverse perspectives |
| Company size | Mix of mid-market and enterprise | Balanced product feedback |
| Role level | VP+ (decision-makers) | Strategic input, not tactical |
| Tenure | 3+ months as customer | Enough experience to provide informed feedback |
| Health score | > 70 (Healthy or above) | Engaged, positive advocates |
| Geographic spread | Multiple US regions | Representative of market |

### Member Roles

| Role | Count | Responsibility |
|------|-------|---------------|
| Chair (Customer) | 1 | Co-facilitate meetings, represent member voice |
| Members | 11–14 | Attend meetings, provide feedback, participate in activities |
| ARG-Builder Executive Sponsor | 1 (CEO) | Set agenda, present vision, listen |
| ARG-Builder Product Lead | 1 (CPO/VP Product) | Present roadmap, gather requirements |
| ARG-Builder Facilitator | 1 (VP CS) | Logistics, follow-up, relationship management |

## Recruitment Strategy

### Ideal CAB Member Profile

| Criterion | Weight | Scoring |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Strategic value of their feedback | 30% | Industry expertise, use case complexity |
| Advocacy potential | 25% | Willingness to reference, speak, write |
| Relationship strength | 20% | Executive engagement, NPS score |
| Diversity contribution | 15% | Industry, size, geography, role |
| Retention risk mitigation | 10% | High-value accounts where deeper engagement prevents churn |

### Recruitment Process

| Step | Action | Owner | Timeline |
|------|--------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Identify 25 candidates from customer base | VP CS | Week 1 |
| 2 | Score and rank candidates against criteria | VP CS + CEO | Week 1 |
| 3 | CEO sends personal invitation email | CEO | Week 2 |
| 4 | Follow-up call to discuss value proposition | VP CS | Week 2–3 |
| 5 | Confirm membership and send welcome package | VP CS | Week 3 |
| 6 | Onboard: expectations, calendar, portal access | VP CS | Week 4 |

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to discuss? I'd love to share more about what we're building and hear your thoughts.

Warm regards,

## Value Exchange

The CAB must deliver genuine value to members — not just extract feedback. The value exchange must feel balanced and generous.

### Value to Members

| Benefit | Description | Delivery |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Early access | 60-day preview of new features | Automatic feature flags |
| Direct influence | Roadmap votes and priority input | Quarterly voting sessions |
| Peer network | Connections with other operational leaders | Private Slack channel, dinners |
| Executive access | Direct line to CEO and product team | Dedicated Slack channel |
| Recognition | Public acknowledgment (with permission) | Website, events, press |
| Professional development | Exclusive content, research, benchmarks | Quarterly reports |
