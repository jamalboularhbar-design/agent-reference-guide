## Follow-Up Sequence

The dinner is the beginning of the relationship, not the end. The following sequence ensures momentum is maintained and converts interest into pipeline.

**Day after dinner (Day +1):** Send a personalized thank-you email referencing something specific they said during the conversation. Include a link to the ROI calculator and offer a 1-on-1 demo.

**Day +3:** Connect on LinkedIn (if not already connected) with a note referencing the dinner. Share the "State of Operational Knowledge" report as a value-add.

**Day +7:** Send a brief email with a specific use case relevant to their company. For example: "Based on what you shared about onboarding challenges at [Company], here's how OpsCanvas handles that..." Include a screenshot or short video.

**Day +14:** Offer the founding customer program (30% discount, locked 24 months). Create urgency by noting the limited spots remaining.

**Day +21:** Final follow-up. If no response, send a brief "no hard feelings" note and offer to stay in touch. Add them to the LinkedIn content audience for long-term nurture.
