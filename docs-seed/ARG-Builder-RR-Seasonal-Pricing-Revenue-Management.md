# Riad & Routes — Seasonal Pricing & Revenue Management

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the revenue management strategy for Riad & Routes, including seasonal pricing tiers, dynamic rate adjustments, distribution channel management, and yield optimization. Effective revenue management ensures we maximize RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room) while maintaining brand positioning as a premium boutique property.

## 2. Season Definitions

| Season | Months | Occupancy Target | Rate Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Peak | March – May, October – November | 90-95% | Maximum rates, minimum 3-night stay |
| High | September, December (1-20) | 80-90% | High rates, 2-night minimum |
| Shoulder | June, January – February | 60-75% | Moderate rates, no minimum stay |
| Low | July – August | 40-60% | Discounted rates, packages promoted |
| Ultra-Peak | Dec 20 – Jan 5, Eid al-Fitr week | 100% | Premium rates, 5-night minimum, no discounts |

## 4. Dynamic Pricing Rules

### 4.1 Occupancy-Based Adjustments

The Revenue Manager adjusts rates based on real-time occupancy for the booking window:

| Occupancy Level (for target date) | Rate Adjustment |
| :--- | :--- |
| Below 40% (14 days out) | Reduce by 15%, activate OTA promotions |
| 40-60% (14 days out) | Standard rate, no adjustment |
| 60-80% (7 days out) | Increase by 10% |
| 80-90% (7 days out) | Increase by 20%, close lowest room category on OTAs |
| Above 90% (any window) | Maximum rate, close all discounts, direct bookings only |

### 4.2 Booking Window Pricing

| Booking Lead Time | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- |
| 90+ days in advance | Early bird discount: -10% (direct bookings only) |
| 30-89 days | Standard published rate |
| 7-29 days | Standard rate or +10% if occupancy > 70% |
| 0-6 days (last minute) | +15% premium OR -20% flash sale (occupancy-dependent) |

### 4.3 Length-of-Stay Incentives

| Stay Duration | Incentive |
| :--- | :--- |
| 1-2 nights | Standard rate |
| 3-4 nights | -5% + complimentary airport transfer |
| 5-6 nights | -10% + transfer + 1 complimentary experience |
| 7+ nights | -15% + transfer + 2 experiences + room upgrade (if available) |

## 5. Distribution Channel Strategy

### 5.1 Channel Mix Targets

| Channel | Target Share | Commission/Cost | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Direct (website + email + phone) | 45% | 0% commission | Highest — always offer best rate |
| Booking.com | 20% | 15-18% commission | Maintain visibility, manage inventory |
| Airbnb Luxe | 10% | 14% commission | Premium positioning |
| Travel advisors & DMCs | 15% | 10-12% commission | Relationship-based, high-value guests |
| TripAdvisor / Viator | 5% | 12-15% commission | Experiences primarily |
| Other OTAs (Expedia, Hotels.com) | 5% | 18-22% commission | Lowest priority, close during peak |

### 5.2 Rate Parity Policy

- Direct booking rate is ALWAYS the best available rate (BAR) or lower
- OTA rates may be equal to but never lower than direct rate
- Direct booking perks (not available on OTAs): free upgrade, late check-out, welcome drink, flexible cancellation
- If a guest finds a lower rate on an OTA, we match it AND add a complimentary experience

### 5.3 Inventory Allocation

- Direct channels: always open, full inventory available
- Booking.com: maximum 60% of inventory allocated (close rooms when occupancy > 80%)
- Airbnb Luxe: premium rooms only (Deluxe and above)
- Travel advisors: full inventory, but must confirm within 48 hours (no indefinite holds)

## 6. Revenue Reporting

### 6.1 Daily Revenue Report (produced by 09:00)

| Metric | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| Rooms sold last night | Occupied rooms / total rooms |
| ADR (Average Daily Rate) | Total room revenue / rooms sold |
| RevPAR | Total room revenue / total available rooms |
| Pickup (next 7 days) | New bookings received yesterday for next 7 days |
| Cancellations | Bookings cancelled yesterday |
| F&B revenue | Total food and beverage revenue yesterday |
| Ancillary revenue | Tours, spa, transfers, minibar |

### 6.2 Monthly Revenue Review (1st of each month)

The GM and Revenue Manager review:
- Month-over-month and year-over-year comparisons
- Channel performance (revenue and commission cost per channel)
- Rate strategy effectiveness (were adjustments profitable?)
- Forecast accuracy (predicted vs actual occupancy)
- Competitive set analysis (how did comparable riads perform?)

Revenue Manager checks competitor rates every Monday and Thursday via:
- Direct website checks
