# Brand Naming Development

## Naming Criteria
- Must convey: operational intelligence, structured knowledge, team empowerment
- Must feel: premium, enterprise-ready, modern (not "wiki" or "docs")
- Must be: memorable, 2-3 syllables max, available as .com or .io
- Must NOT sound like: a note-taking app, a generic wiki, or a chatbot

## Naming Directions

### Direction 1: "Command" Metaphor (Authority + Control)
The operations leader commands their domain with structured knowledge.

| Name | Tagline | Rationale |
|------|---------|-----------|
| **Runbook** | "Your operations, always ready." | Direct reference to ops playbooks. Strong verb. Memorable. |
| **Cadence** | "Operational rhythm for growing teams." | Implies consistency, process, flow. Musical/premium feel. |
| **Playbook AI** | "The AI-powered operational playbook." | Instantly understood. Category-defining. |

### Direction 2: "Clarity" Metaphor (Insight + Structure)
Cutting through chaos to deliver clear operational knowledge.

| Name | Tagline | Rationale |
|------|---------|-----------|
| **Clarion** | "Clear signals for every team." | Means "a clear, loud call." Premium sound. Unique. |
| **Lumen** | "Illuminate how your company runs." | Light metaphor. Short. Elegant. Scientific feel. |
| **Prism** | "Every angle of your operations, in focus." | Refracts complexity into clarity. Visual. |

### Direction 3: "Foundation" Metaphor (Structure + Reliability)
The bedrock that teams build on.

| Name | Tagline | Rationale |
|------|---------|-----------|
| **Basecamp** (taken) | — | — |
| **Keystn** | "The keystone of operational excellence." | Architectural. Essential. Short. |
| **Pillar** | "The foundation your teams stand on." | Strong. Simple. Enterprise-friendly. |

### Direction 4: "Intelligence" Metaphor (AI + Smart Ops)
AI-native operational intelligence platform.

| Name | Tagline | Rationale |
|------|---------|-----------|
| **OpusAI** | "Orchestrate operational excellence." | Opus = great work. AI suffix signals modernity. |
| **Synaptic** | "Connected knowledge for connected teams." | Neural/brain metaphor. AI-native feel. |
| **Cortex** | "The operational brain for your company." | Direct brain metaphor. Strong. Memorable. |

### Direction 5: "Navigator" Metaphor (Guidance + Journey)
Guiding teams through complex operations.

| Name | Tagline | Rationale |
|------|---------|-----------|
| **Compass** | "Point your team in the right direction." | Universal navigation metaphor. Clear. |
| **Meridian** | "Navigate operational complexity with confidence." | Premium. Geographic. Precise. |
| **Waymark** | "Mark the way for every team member." | Trail/path metaphor. Friendly but professional. |

---

## Top 3 Recommendations

### 1. **Meridian** ⭐ (Top Pick)
- **Full name:** Meridian
- **Tagline:** "Navigate operational complexity with confidence."
- **Why:** Premium sound. Unique in the KM space. Implies precision, direction, and global scale. Works for mid-market AND enterprise. No direct competitor uses this name. Evokes the idea of a reference line — exactly what an operational guide is.
- **Domain:** meridian.io or getmeridian.com likely available

### 2. **Cortex**
- **Full name:** Cortex
- **Tagline:** "The operational brain for your company."
- **Why:** Strong AI connotation. Memorable. One word. Implies intelligence and processing. However, there are some companies using this name in adjacent spaces.

### 3. **Cadence**
- **Full name:** Cadence
- **Tagline:** "Operational rhythm for growing teams."
- **Why:** Beautiful word. Implies consistency and process. Musical
