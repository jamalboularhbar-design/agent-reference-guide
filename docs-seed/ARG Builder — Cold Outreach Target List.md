## Outreach Sequence Template

**Subject Line Options:**

- "Your team spends 9.3 hours/week searching for answers — here's the fix"

- "[Company] + ARG Builder: operational intelligence for [vertical]"

- "Quick question about [Company]'s knowledge management"

Hi [First Name],

I noticed [Company] is scaling fast — [specific observation about their growth, hiring, or expansion].

Quick question: how does your team handle operational knowledge today? SOPs, playbooks, onboarding docs — where do they live?

I ask because I built ARG Builder specifically for mid-market [vertical] companies that have outgrown Google Docs but can't justify Service Now. We turn scattered operational knowledge into a living intelligence layer with AI governance, knowledge graphs, and reading analytics.

Would a 15-minute demo be worth your time this week?
