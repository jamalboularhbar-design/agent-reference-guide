## Full Description

### The Problem

Mid-market companies (200–2,000 employees) lose an average of $4.3 million per year because their operational knowledge is scattered across disconnected tools. Employees spend 9.3 hours per week searching for information they already have. Legacy tools like Notion and Confluence were built for writing — not for governing operational knowledge at scale.

### The Solution

ARG Builder is the AI-powered operational knowledge platform that turns chaos into clarity:

**AI Content Governance** — Automatically flags stale documents, detects duplicates, and suggests improvements. Your knowledge base stays healthy without manual audits.

**Interactive Knowledge Graph** — Visualize how all your operational documents connect. Find gaps before they become incidents. Understand dependencies at a glance.

**Reading Analytics** — Real-time heatmaps showing who read what, when, and how deeply. Prove compliance. Measure adoption. Know which teams are falling behind.

**AI Document Generation** — Don't start from a blank page. Generate structured SOPs, playbooks, and guides from prompts, then customize for your team.

**Dual Persona System** — Different teams see different operational views. Your engineering team's runbooks don't clutter your HR team's onboarding guides.

### What Makes Us Different

Unlike generic wikis (Notion, Confluence) or narrow tools (Trainual, Guru):
- We don't just store documents — we govern them with AI
- We don't just track page views — we measure reading depth and comprehension
- We don't just organize content — we map knowledge connections
- We're 40% cheaper than Guru and include 10x more features than Trainual

### Built, Not Pitched

ARG Builder is a production-grade platform with 92 database tables, 109 application pages, 307 passing tests, and 525+ pre-loaded operational documents. This isn't a prototype or an MVP — it's ready for enterprise deployment today.

- **Starter:** $299/month flat (up to 25 users) — 14-day free trial
- **Professional:** $15/user/month (25-seat minimum) — full feature set
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for 500+ employees
