# ARG Builder — Weekly Metrics Dashboard

**Purpose:** A structured template for tracking key business metrics every Monday morning. Copy this into a spreadsheet or Notion database and update weekly to maintain visibility into launch progress, pipeline health, and product adoption.

---

## Dashboard Structure

The dashboard is organized into four quadrants: Acquisition (how prospects enter the funnel), Activation (how they convert to customers), Revenue (financial health), and Product (platform usage and health). Each metric has a target for the first 90 days post-launch.

## Weekly Review Process

Every Monday morning (9:00 AM), spend 30 minutes updating this dashboard. The process follows a consistent rhythm: pull data from Stripe (revenue metrics), pull data from the platform analytics API (product metrics), check LinkedIn analytics (acquisition metrics), and review the CRM pipeline (activation metrics).

After updating the numbers, write a brief 3-sentence summary answering three questions: What went well this week? What didn't go as planned? What's the single most important action for next week?

---

## Status Indicators

Use these status indicators for quick visual scanning:

- **On Track** — Metric is at or above the 90-day target pace
- **Watch** — Metric is within 20% of target but trending in the wrong direction
- **At Risk** — Metric is more than 20% below target pace and needs immediate attention
- **Exceeding** — Metric is significantly above target (investigate why and double down)

---

## Monthly Roll-Up

At the end of each month, aggregate the weekly data into a monthly summary. The monthly roll-up feeds into the investor update email (if fundraising) and the quarterly board/advisor meeting deck. Key monthly metrics to highlight: MRR and growth rate, total customers and net adds, CAC and payback period, NPS and retention, and the single biggest learning or insight from the month.

---

## Milestone Triggers

When specific metrics hit threshold values, they trigger next-phase actions:

| Milestone | Trigger | Action |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| First $1K MRR | 3–4 customers signed | Begin Product Hunt launch preparation |
| First $5K MRR | 10+ customers | Begin hiring Head of Sales |
| 20 founding customers | Spots filled | Close founding program, raise prices to standard |
| NPS > 60 | Sustained for 4 weeks | Launch referral program actively |
| Demo-to-close > 40% | Sustained for 4 weeks | Begin scaling outbound volume (hire SDR) |
| Churn > 10% | Any month | Pause acquisition, focus on retention and product fixes |
