# ARG-Builder: Partnerships & Ecosystem Development

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's partnership and ecosystem strategy — the systematic approach to building a network of strategic relationships that accelerate growth, deepen product value, and create compounding competitive advantages. Partnerships are not bolt-on activities; they are core to the business model and long-term defensibility.

| Service model | Advisory + implementation (not just resale) |
