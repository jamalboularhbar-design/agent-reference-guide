# ARG-Builder: Product Launch Playbook

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's product launch methodology — the repeatable, cross-functional process for bringing new features, products, and major updates to market with maximum impact. A structured launch process ensures alignment across product, engineering, marketing, sales, and customer success, creating coordinated momentum that drives adoption and revenue.

---

## Launch Tier Framework

### Launch Classification

| Tier | Criteria | Examples | Lead Time | Cross-Functional Effort |
|------|----------|---------|-----------|------------------------|
| **Tier 1 (Major)** | New product, major platform change, new market entry | New product line, platform redesign, enterprise launch | 8–12 weeks | Full company |
| **Tier 2 (Significant)** | Major feature, new integration, pricing change | AI capabilities, API platform, new tier | 4–6 weeks | Marketing + Sales + CS |
| **Tier 3 (Standard)** | Feature enhancement, improvement, minor new feature | Search upgrade, new template, UX improvement | 2–3 weeks | Marketing + CS |
| **Tier 4 (Minor)** | Bug fix, small improvement, maintenance | Performance fix, UI tweak, copy change | 0–1 week | Release notes only |

## Launch Communication Templates

### Internal Announcement (All-Hands)

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| What we're launching | Clear description of the product/feature |
| Why it matters | Customer problem solved, market opportunity |
| Who it's for | Target audience and use cases |
| Key messaging | 3 main talking points for everyone |
| Timeline | Key dates and milestones |
| How you can help | Specific asks by department |
| Resources | Links to assets, training, documentation |

### Customer Announcement Email

| Element | Content | Best Practice |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| Subject line | Benefit-focused, curiosity-driving | A/B test 2 variants |
| Preview text | Expand on subject, add urgency | 40–90 characters |
| Hero section | Visual + headline + 1-line value prop | Above the fold |
| Body | 3 key benefits with brief descriptions | Scannable, benefit-led |
| Social proof | Quote from beta customer | Named, with title |
| CTA | Single, clear action | "Try it now" / "See it in action" |
| P.S. | Secondary offer or urgency | Optional |
