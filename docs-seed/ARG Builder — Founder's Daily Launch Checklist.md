# ARG Builder — Founder's Daily Launch Checklist

**Purpose:** A structured daily routine for the first 30 days of launch (June–July 2026) to maximize momentum, pipeline generation, and founding customer acquisition.

**Goal:** 5 founding customers signed by August 31, 2026 → $5K+ MRR

---

## Daily Rhythm (2.5 hours of dedicated GTM work)

### Morning Block (8:00–9:00 AM) — Outreach & Content

| # | Task | Time | Notes |
|---|------|------|-------|
| 1 | Check email replies from yesterday's outreach | 10 min | Respond to warm leads within 1 hour |
| 2 | Send 5 new cold emails (from personalized sequences) | 15 min | Use the Cold-Emails-Top5 templates, personalize for each |
| 3 | Send 5 LinkedIn connection requests | 10 min | Use Template A, B, or C from the target list |
| 4 | Engage with 3 LinkedIn posts from target prospects | 10 min | Thoughtful comments, not generic "great post!" |
| 5 | Post or schedule LinkedIn content (if posting day) | 15 min | Mon/Wed/Fri posting schedule |

### Midday Block (12:00–12:30 PM) — Pipeline Management

| # | Task | Time | Notes |
|---|------|------|-------|
| 6 | Update Notion Sales Pipeline with new leads/status changes | 10 min | Move leads through stages, add notes |
| 7 | Follow up on any demo requests or trial signups | 10 min | Respond same-day, propose specific times |
| 8 | Review website analytics (visits, lead captures) | 5 min | Check argbuilder.io traffic and conversion |
| 9 | Check Stripe dashboard for any new signups | 5 min | Celebrate wins, follow up on abandoned checkouts |

### Afternoon Block (4:00–5:00 PM) — Relationship Building

| # | Task | Time | Notes |
|---|------|------|-------|
| 10 | Conduct any scheduled demos or calls | 30 min | Use the 30-min demo script |
| 11 | Send post-demo follow-ups (within 2 hours of call) | 15 min | Include One-Pager, ROI calc, proposed next steps |
| 12 | Research tomorrow's outreach targets on LinkedIn | 15 min | Find specific hooks, recent posts, company news |

## Weekly Cadence

### Monday — Fresh Start

- Review last week's metrics (emails sent, replies, demos, signups)
- Plan the week's 25 cold emails (5 per day)
- Publish LinkedIn Post (thought leadership)
- Update the Notion Launch Command Center with weekly goals

### Tuesday — Deep Outreach

- Focus on Email 2 follow-ups (value-add with ROI numbers)
- Research 5 new target companies to add to the pipeline
- Engage in 2 LinkedIn group discussions about knowledge management

### Wednesday — Content & Visibility

- Publish LinkedIn Post (product insight or industry data)
- Write one short-form article or thread about operational knowledge
- Send LinkedIn DMs to anyone who engaged with your content

### Thursday — Demos & Closing

- Prioritize demo calls (stack them on Thursdays if possible)
- Send Email 3 (dinner invitation) to non-responders from last week
- Review and update the Founding Customer Agreement for any active negotiations

### Friday — Reflection & Planning

- Publish LinkedIn Post (founder journey or behind-the-scenes)
- Review the week: what worked, what didn't, what to change
- Update all pipeline stages in Notion
- Plan next week's personalized outreach (research targets over weekend)
- Send a "weekend read" email to warm leads (article or insight, no pitch)

## Daily Non-Negotiables

These 5 actions happen every single day, no exceptions:

1. **Send 5 outreach messages** (email or LinkedIn) — pipeline never stops
2. **Respond to all inbound within 2 hours** — speed kills in early-stage sales
3. **Update Notion pipeline** — if it's not tracked, it didn't happen
4. **Engage on LinkedIn** (even if not posting) — stay visible to prospects
5. **End the day with tomorrow's targets researched** — never start cold in the morning
