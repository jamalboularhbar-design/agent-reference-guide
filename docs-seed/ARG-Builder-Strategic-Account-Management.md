# ARG-Builder: Strategic Account Management

## Framework for Growing and Retaining Your Most Valuable Customer Relationships

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Strategic accounts represent disproportionate revenue, reference value, and growth potential. They require a fundamentally different approach than standard customer success — one that combines executive relationship management, multi-threaded engagement, proactive value creation, and long-term partnership thinking. This document defines ARG-Builder's strategic account management (SAM) program for the top 10–20% of accounts that drive 50–70% of revenue.

## 7. Risk Management & Retention

### 7.1 Risk Indicators

| Risk Category | Signals | Severity | Response |
|-------------|---------|----------|----------|
| Usage decline | DAU/WAU dropping > 20% | High | Immediate CSM outreach |
| Champion departure | Key contact leaves company | Critical | Identify new champion within 2 weeks |
| Executive change | New VP/C-level in buying org | High | Executive introduction within 30 days |
| Support escalations | Multiple P1 tickets in 30 days | High | Technical review + executive apology |
| Competitive evaluation | Customer evaluating alternatives | Critical | Competitive response plan |
| Budget pressure | Customer mentions cost concerns | Medium | Value reinforcement + creative structuring |
| Merger/acquisition | Customer being acquired | High | Relationship mapping in new org |
| Strategic misalignment | Customer's direction diverging from our product | Medium | Roadmap discussion, honest assessment |

### 7.2 Save Playbook

| Risk Level | Actions | Timeline | Escalation |
|-----------|---------|----------|-----------|
| Yellow (early warning) | CSM proactive outreach, value review | 1 week | CS Manager aware |
| Orange (at risk) | Executive engagement, action plan, concessions | 2 weeks | VP CS + Executive Sponsor |
| Red (likely to churn) | All-hands save effort, executive-to-executive | Immediate | CEO involvement |

### 7.3 Competitive Defense

| Situation | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Customer taking competitor meeting | Request equal time, prepare competitive brief |
| Customer sharing competitor pricing | Value comparison, TCO analysis, don't match price |
| Customer requesting features competitor has | Roadmap commitment (if genuine), workaround |
| Customer citing competitor case study | Provide relevant counter-examples |
| Customer in formal RFP (re-evaluation) | Full RFP response, executive engagement, references |
