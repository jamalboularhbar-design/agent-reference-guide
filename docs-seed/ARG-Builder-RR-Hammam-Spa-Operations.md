## 5. Booking & Scheduling

### 5.1 Booking Process

1. Guest requests treatment via: front desk, WhatsApp, or in-room tablet
2. Spa Attendant checks availability and confirms within 15 minutes
3. Guest is asked about: preferred time, any health conditions, pressure preference (massage)
4. Confirmation message sent with: treatment name, time, duration, what to bring (nothing — we provide everything)
5. Reminder message sent 1 hour before appointment

### 5.2 Scheduling Rules

- Minimum 30 minutes between treatments in the same room (for cleaning and setup)
- Maximum 3 consecutive treatments per therapist (mandatory 30-min break after 3)
- VIP guests receive priority scheduling (can bump standard bookings with 4h notice)
- Walk-in availability: always keep 1 slot open per half-day for spontaneous requests
- No treatments scheduled during the first 30 minutes after a guest's check-in (allow settling time)

### 5.3 Cancellation Policy

| :--- | :--- |

| 2-4 hours before | 50% charge |

### 6.2 Contraindications & Safety

Before every treatment, the therapist must verbally confirm:

- "Do you have any heart conditions or high blood pressure?" (contraindication for hammam)
- "Are you pregnant?" (modify treatment — no hot stones, lighter pressure, shorter steam)
- "Do you have any skin conditions, open wounds, or recent sunburn?" (avoid affected areas)
- "Any allergies to nuts?" (argan oil is nut-derived)

If any contraindication is identified, the therapist modifies the treatment or suggests an alternative. Never proceed with a contraindicated treatment.

## 7. Hygiene & Sanitation

### 7.1 Between Each Guest

| Item | Action | Product |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Hammam marble slab | Scrub with antibacterial solution, rinse, dry | Hospital-grade sanitizer |
| Massage table | Wipe with disinfectant, fresh sheet and towel | Surface disinfectant |
| Kessa gloves | Single-use per guest (disposed after) | — |
| Towels & robes | Fresh set per guest (never reused without washing) | — |
| Slippers | Disposable pair per guest | — |
| Water bowls (hammam) | Emptied, rinsed, refilled with fresh water | — |

### 7.2 Daily Deep Clean (21:00 – 21:30)

- All surfaces mopped with antibacterial floor cleaner
- Hammam walls and ceiling scrubbed (prevent mold)
- Drain covers removed and cleaned
- All product bottles wiped down
- Fresh candles placed, old ones discarded
- Towel inventory counted and restocked from laundry

### 7.3 Weekly Maintenance

- Hammam steam generator descaled
- Water quality tested (pH, chlorine in plunge pool)
- Essential oil diffusers cleaned
- Massage oil warmers inspected
- Fire extinguisher check (candle risk)
