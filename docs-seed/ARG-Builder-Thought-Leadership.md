### 3.3 LinkedIn Strategy (CEO)

**Posting Framework (3x/week):**

**Monday — Insight Post:**
- Share a counterintuitive observation about operations/AI
- Format: Hook → Context → Insight → Implication → Question
- Length: 150–300 words

**Wednesday — Story Post:**
- Customer success story or personal founder journey
- Format: Situation → Challenge → Action → Result → Lesson
- Length: 200–400 words

**Friday — Framework Post:**
- Share a mental model or framework
- Format: Problem → Framework visual → How to apply → Results
- Length: 100–250 words + image/carousel

**Engagement Rules:**
- Respond to every comment within 4 hours
- Comment on 5 relevant posts daily
- Share and add perspective to industry news
- Tag relevant people (not spam)
- Never hard-sell in thought leadership content

### 3.4 Original Research Program

**Annual Research Reports:**

**Report 1: "State of Operational Intelligence" (Q1)**

**Report 2: "AI Operations Benchmark" (Q3)**

**Research Process:**
1. Design survey (Month 1)
2. Field survey — 500+ respondents (Month 2)
3. Analyze data + write report (Month 3)
4. Design + produce final report (Month 3)
5. Launch campaign (Month 4)
6. Ongoing promotion (Months 4–6)
