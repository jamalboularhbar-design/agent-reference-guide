# ARG-Builder: Customer Onboarding Playbook

## Executive Summary

This playbook defines the end-to-end customer onboarding process for ARG-Builder, from contract signature through full deployment and value realization. The onboarding experience is the single most important driver of long-term retention — customers who achieve "first value" within 14 days retain at 95%+ rates, while those who take longer than 30 days churn at 3x the average rate. This playbook ensures every customer reaches full deployment within 14 days with measurable adoption metrics exceeding 85%.

---

## Onboarding Philosophy

ARG-Builder's onboarding is built on three principles: **speed to value** (first meaningful output within 48 hours), **guided autonomy** (customers feel empowered, not dependent), and **measurable outcomes** (every milestone has a quantifiable success metric). The onboarding process mirrors the product's own philosophy — structured, efficient, and outcome-driven.

## Onboarding Stages

### Stage 1: Welcome & Kickoff (Day 0–1)

**Trigger:** Contract signed in CRM

**Automated Actions (within 1 hour of signature):**

| Action | Channel | Owner |
|--------|---------|-------|
| Welcome email with login credentials | Email | System |
| Slack channel creation (shared with customer) | Slack | System |
| Calendar invite for kickoff call | Calendar | CSM |
| Internal handoff from Sales to CS | CRM | System |
| Customer record created in onboarding tracker | Dashboard | System |

**Welcome Email Template:**

Subject: Welcome to ARG-Builder — Let's Build Your Operational System

Body:
- Congratulations and excitement about partnership
- Login credentials and platform access link
- 90-second "Getting Started" video link
- Kickoff call confirmation (date/time)
- Direct contact information for their CSM
- What to prepare before the kickoff call

**Kickoff Call Agenda (45 minutes):**

| Segment | Duration | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Introductions | 5 min | Build rapport, confirm stakeholders |
| Success definition | 10 min | Agree on measurable outcomes |
| Business context | 10 min | Understand personas, processes, pain points |
| Platform walkthrough | 10 min | Demonstrate key features relevant to their use case |
| Onboarding plan review | 5 min | Confirm timeline, milestones, responsibilities |
| Q&A and next steps | 5 min | Address concerns, assign homework |

**Kickoff Call Deliverables:**

| Deliverable | Owner | Due |
|-------------|-------|-----|
| Success criteria document | CSM | Day 1 |
| Persona intake questionnaire sent | CSM | Day 1 |
| Access provisioned for all stakeholders | Customer | Day 2 |
| Business context summary | CSM | Day 1 |

### Stage 2: Discovery & Configuration (Days 2–5)

**Objective:** Gather all business information needed to generate the operational reference guide. This is where ARG-Builder's AI does the heavy lifting.

**Discovery Process:**

| Method | Duration | Purpose |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Persona intake questionnaire | 30 min (async) | Define roles, responsibilities, processes |
| AI-guided interview (per persona) | 20 min each | Deep-dive into workflows and decision trees |
| Document upload | Async | Existing SOPs, playbooks, org charts |
| Stakeholder validation call | 30 min | Confirm AI-generated content accuracy |

**Persona Intake Questionnaire (sent Day 1, due Day 3):**

| Section | Questions | Purpose |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Business Overview | Industry, size, structure, goals | Context setting |
| Persona Definitions | Roles, responsibilities, communication style | Persona creation |
| Process Flows | Key workflows, stages, handoffs | Process documentation |
| Pain Points | Current challenges, failed solutions | Priority setting |
| Success Metrics | How they'll measure ROI | Outcome alignment |
| Existing Documentation | SOPs, playbooks, training materials | Content acceleration |

**AI Interview Sessions:**

The ARG-Builder AI conducts structured interviews with key stakeholders to extract operational knowledge. Each interview is 20 minutes, conducted via the platform's built-in interview module.

| Interview | Participant | Focus |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Interview 1 | CEO/Founder | Vision, strategy, decision-making |
| Interview 2 | Operations Lead | Day-to-day processes, bottlenecks |
| Interview 3 | Team Lead(s) | Execution details, edge cases |
| Interview 4 | Customer-facing role | Client interactions, service delivery |

**Configuration Checklist:**

| Item | Status | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Company branding uploaded (logo, colors) | ☐ | |
| All personas defined (min 2, max 6) | ☐ | |
| Process flows documented (min 5 stages each) | ☐ | |
| Team members invited to platform | ☐ | |
| Success metrics confirmed | ☐ | |
| Design preferences selected | ☐ | |

### Stage 3: Guide Generation & Review (Days 5–8)

**Objective:** ARG-Builder's AI generates the complete operational reference guide. Customer reviews and provides feedback for refinement.

**Generation Process:**

| Step | Duration | Output |
|------|----------|--------|
| AI content generation | 12–19 hours | Full guide draft |
| Internal QA review | 2 hours | Quality-checked draft |
| Customer review period | 48 hours | Feedback and corrections |
| Revision cycle | 4–8 hours | Final guide |

**Customer Review Guidelines:**

Provide the customer with a structured review framework to ensure feedback is actionable and comprehensive.

| Review Area | Questions to Consider |
|-------------|----------------------|
| Accuracy | Does this accurately reflect how your team operates? |
| Completeness | Is anything missing from the processes or personas? |
| Tone | Does the communication style match your brand? |
| Priority | Are the most important processes highlighted appropriately? |
| Terminology | Are industry-specific terms used correctly? |

**Revision SLA:**

| Revision Type | Turnaround | Included |
|---------------|-----------|----------|
| Content corrections | 4 hours | Unlimited |
| Process additions | 8 hours | Up to 3 per persona |
| Persona additions | 12 hours | Up to 2 additional |
| Complete restructure | 24 hours | 1 per onboarding |

### Stage 4: Deployment & Team Activation (Days 8–12)

**Objective:** Deploy the guide to the full team and achieve initial adoption. This is the critical moment — the guide must be introduced with context and enthusiasm, not just "here's a new tool."

**Deployment Sequence:**

| Day | Activity | Owner |
|-----|----------|-------|
| Day 8 | Final guide approved by customer champion | Customer |
| Day 9 | Team announcement email sent | CSM + Customer |
| Day 9 | Platform access provisioned for all team members | CSM |
| Day 10 | Team training session (30 min) | CSM |
| Day 11 | Follow-up: answer questions, address concerns | CSM |
| Day 12 | Adoption check: verify all team members have logged in | CSM |

**Team Training Session (30 minutes):**

| Segment | Duration | Content |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Why this exists | 5 min | Problem it solves, leadership commitment |
| How to use it | 10 min | Navigation, search, command palette, timelines |
| Live demonstration | 10 min | Walk through a real scenario using the guide |
| Q&A | 5 min | Address concerns, gather feedback |

**Team Announcement Email Template:**

Subject: Your New Operational Playbook is Live — Here's How to Use It

Key elements:
- Leadership endorsement (quote from CEO/COO)
- What it is and why it matters (2 sentences)
- How to access (direct link)
- What to do first (specific action)
- Training session invitation
- Support contact

### Stage 5: Value Realization & Handoff (Days 12–14)

**Objective:** Confirm the customer has achieved "first value" — measurable improvement in at least one operational metric — and transition from onboarding to ongoing customer success.

**First Value Metrics:**

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Team adoption rate | > 85% | Unique logins / total team members |
| Daily active usage | > 40% | DAU / total users |
| Search queries | > 5/user/week | Platform analytics |
| Process adherence | Qualitative | Customer feedback |
| Time savings | > 2 hours/week/person | Customer survey |

**Handoff Meeting (30 minutes):**

| Segment | Duration | Content |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Adoption metrics review | 10 min | Share dashboard, celebrate wins |
| Value confirmation | 5 min | Customer confirms measurable improvement |
| Ongoing support introduction | 5 min | Introduce ongoing CSM cadence |
| Growth opportunities | 5 min | Preview expansion features |
| Feedback collection | 5 min | NPS, onboarding satisfaction |

**Handoff Checklist:**

| Item | Status |
|------|--------|
| Adoption rate > 85% confirmed | ☐ |
| Customer champion satisfied (NPS > 8) | ☐ |
| All team members trained | ☐ |
| Ongoing cadence scheduled (monthly/quarterly) | ☐ |
| Expansion opportunities identified | ☐ |
| Onboarding satisfaction survey completed | ☐ |
| Internal case study data captured | ☐ |
