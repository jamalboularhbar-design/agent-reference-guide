# ARG-Builder: Customer Segmentation Model

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's customer segmentation strategy — the data-driven framework for dividing the total addressable market and existing customer base into distinct groups that enable personalized experiences, targeted marketing, optimized sales motions, and tailored product development. Effective segmentation is the foundation of efficient go-to-market execution.

|---------|----------------|-----------|-------------|----------|

## Lifecycle Segmentation

### Customer Journey Stages

| Stage | Definition | Duration | Primary Goal | Owner |
|-------|-----------|----------|-------------|-------|
| **Prospect** | Identified, not yet engaged | Variable | Generate interest | Marketing |
| **Evaluating** | Active trial or evaluation | 14–30 days | Convert to paid | Sales |
| **Onboarding** | New customer, implementing | 0–30 days | Achieve first value | CS |
| **Adopting** | Active, growing usage | 30–90 days | Deepen engagement | CS |
| **Expanding** | Mature, ready for growth | 90+ days | Increase ACV | CS + Sales |
| **Renewing** | Approaching renewal | 90 days before renewal | Secure renewal | CS |
