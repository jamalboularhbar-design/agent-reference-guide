# ARG-Builder: Employee Handbook & HR Policies

## Executive Summary

This document defines ARG-Builder's employee handbook — the comprehensive guide to company policies, benefits, expectations, and culture that every team member needs to succeed. A well-crafted handbook protects both the company and employees, sets clear expectations, and reinforces the culture we are building. This handbook is designed for a high-growth, remote-first SaaS startup scaling from 5 to 75+ employees.

## Performance Management

### Performance Review Cycle

| Activity | Frequency | Participants | Output |
|----------|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Goal setting (OKRs) | Quarterly | Manager + employee | Documented goals |
| Check-in conversations | Bi-weekly | Manager + employee | Notes in 1:1 doc |
| Peer feedback | Quarterly | 3–5 peers selected | Anonymous feedback |
| Formal review | Semi-annually | Manager + employee | Written review + rating |
| Compensation review | Annually | Manager + HR + Finance | Merit increase decision |

### Performance Ratings

| Rating | Definition | Distribution Target | Compensation Impact |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Exceptional | Consistently exceeds all expectations | 10–15% | 8–15% increase + equity refresh |
| Strong | Meets and often exceeds expectations | 30–40% | 5–8% increase |
| Solid | Consistently meets expectations | 35–45% | 3–5% increase |
| Developing | Partially meets expectations, improving | 10–15% | 0–3% increase + PIP consideration |
| Below expectations | Does not meet expectations | 0–5% | PIP required |

### Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)

| Phase | Duration | Actions | Outcome |
|-------|----------|---------|---------|
| Verbal feedback | 2 weeks | Clear expectations communicated | Improvement or escalation |
| Written PIP | 30 days | Documented goals, weekly check-ins | Meet goals or separation |
| Extension (rare) | 15 days | Only if significant progress shown | Final determination |

## Separation & Offboarding

### Voluntary Resignation

| Step | Timeline | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| Notice | 2 weeks standard (4 weeks for leadership) | Written resignation to manager |
| Transition plan | Within 2 days of notice | Manager + employee create plan |
| Knowledge transfer | During notice period | Document processes, train replacement |
| Exit interview | Last week | HR conducts confidential interview |
| Final day | Last day | Return equipment, access revoked, final pay |

### Involuntary Termination

| Type | Notice | Severance | Conditions |
|------|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Performance (post-PIP) | Same day | 2 weeks | Signed separation agreement |
| Position elimination | 2 weeks | 4 weeks + COBRA | Signed separation agreement |
| Misconduct | Immediate | None | Investigation complete |
| Reduction in force | 2 weeks | 4–8 weeks (tenure-based) | WARN Act compliance |
