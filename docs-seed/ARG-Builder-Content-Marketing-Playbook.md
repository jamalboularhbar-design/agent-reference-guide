# ARG-Builder: Content Marketing Playbook

## Comprehensive Strategy for Demand Generation Through Thought Leadership Content

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Content marketing is ARG-Builder's most efficient demand generation engine — building brand awareness, establishing thought leadership, generating organic traffic, and nurturing prospects through the buyer journey. Unlike paid acquisition, content compounds over time, creating an ever-growing moat of organic visibility. This playbook defines the complete content marketing strategy from ideation to distribution to measurement.

### 3.2 Content Quality Standards

| Standard | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Originality | No AI-generated filler; unique insights, data, or perspectives |
| Accuracy | All claims backed by data, sources cited |
| Actionability | Reader can implement advice immediately |
| Depth | Comprehensive coverage, not surface-level |
| Voice | Authoritative yet approachable, no jargon without explanation |
| Visual quality | Custom graphics, not stock photos |
| SEO optimization | Target keyword, meta description, internal links |
| Readability | Scannable, clear headings, short paragraphs |
| Mobile-friendly | Responsive formatting, readable on all devices |
