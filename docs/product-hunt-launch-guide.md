# ARG Builder — Product Hunt Launch Guide

**Target Launch:** Tuesday or Wednesday (highest traffic days)  
**Launch Time:** 12:01 AM PST (Product Hunt resets daily)  
**Goal:** Top 5 Product of the Day

## Pre-Launch Checklist (1 Week Before)

### Assets to Prepare

| Asset | Spec | Status |
|-------|------|--------|
| Tagline | Max 60 chars | "AI-powered operational intelligence for mid-market teams" |
| Description | 260 chars max | See below |
| Gallery images | 1270x760px, 5-8 images | Screenshots of Dashboard, Knowledge Graph, Analytics, Landing Page |
| Logo | 240x240px PNG | ARG Builder sparkle logo |
| Thumbnail | 240x240px | Same as logo |
| Maker video | 1-2 min Loom | Use the 60s demo script |
| First comment | 400-600 words | Founder story + what makes ARG Builder different |

### Description (260 chars)
> ARG Builder turns scattered SOPs and tribal knowledge into AI-powered operational intelligence. Purpose-built for mid-market teams across 6 verticals. Deploy in days, not months. Starting at $299/mo.

## First Comment Template

```
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm [Name], founder of ARG Builder.

After running operations for a luxury travel company, I discovered that 
mid-market teams all share the same problem: operational knowledge is 
scattered across Google Docs, Notion, email, and people's heads.

Enterprise tools like Confluence are too complex. Generic wikis don't 
understand your workflows. We built ARG Builder to fill this gap.

**What makes ARG Builder different:**

1. **Industry-specific** — Pre-configured for hospitality, healthcare, 
   professional services, retail, manufacturing, and SaaS
2. **AI-native** — Not a wiki with AI bolted on. AI powers search, 
   content governance, gap detection, and duplicate finding
3. **Deployed in days** — Import from Google Docs, Notion, or Confluence. 
   AI auto-categorizes everything
4. **Built for mid-market** — Enterprise-grade without enterprise complexity 
   or pricing

**What we'd love feedback on:**
- Which vertical should we prioritize next?
- What integrations matter most to your team?

Try it free for 14 days. No credit card required.

Thanks for checking us out! 🙏
```
